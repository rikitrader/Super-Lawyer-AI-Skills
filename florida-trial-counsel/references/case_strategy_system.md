## Timeline Reference

### Streamlined Track (Simple cases)
- Total duration: 6-9 months
- Limited discovery
- Early settlement encouraged

### General Track (Standard cases)
- Total duration: 12-18 months
- Full discovery
- Summary judgment opportunity

### Complex Track (Multi-party/complex issues)
- Total duration: 18-36 months
- Extended discovery
- Dedicated case management

### Foreclosure Track
- Residential: 12-24 months
- Commercial: 18-36 months
- Contested: Add 6-12 months

## System Classification Criteria

### A-System (Aggressive Offense)

**Criteria:**
- Clear liability on documentary evidence
- Undisputed or well-documented damages
- Strong legal authority
- No significant affirmative defenses
- Favorable venue/judge

**Indicators:**
- Win probability: 70%+
- Settlement leverage: High
- Summary judgment: Likely viable

### B-System (Balanced Approach)

**Criteria:**
- Mixed facts requiring credibility determinations
- Disputed elements on key issues
- Competing legal authority
- Viable defenses exist
- Neutral venue/judge

**Indicators:**
- Win probability: 40-70%
- Settlement leverage: Moderate
- Summary judgment: Uncertain

### C-System (Conservative Defense)

**Criteria:**
- Challenging facts or credibility issues
- Significant legal hurdles
- Strong opposing defenses or claims
- Damages speculative or difficult to prove
- Unfavorable venue/judge

**Indicators:**
- Win probability: <40%
- Settlement leverage: Low
- Summary judgment: Opponent may obtain

## Generating Strategic Blueprint

When user requests case analysis, generate complete Strategic Blueprint with:

1. **Executive Summary** - Quick overview with classification
2. **Timeline** - Phase-by-phase duration estimates
3. **Costs** - Attorney vs. Pro Se comparison
4. **System Classification** - A/B/C with rationale
5. **Judge Analysis** - Profile and adjustments (if judge known)
6. **Game Plan** - Step-by-step action plan
7. **Key Decisions** - Major strategic choices
8. **Critical Filings** - Priority-ranked filing list
9. **Risk Assessment** - Strengths/weaknesses/mitigation
10. **Settlement Analysis** - Ranges and timing
