### General Civil Division

**Filing Requirements:**
- E-Portal filing mandatory
- Proposed orders required with motions
- Courtesy copies per judge preference

**Hearing Procedures:**
- Uniform Motion Calendar (UMC) for non-evidentiary matters
- Special set hearings for evidentiary matters
- Emergency procedures through duty judge

**Discovery:**
- Conferral certificate required for all discovery motions
- ESI protocols per administrative order

### Foreclosure Division

**Case Management:**
- Rule 1.115 compliance mandatory
- Mediation referral procedures
- Summary judgment calendar procedures

## Case Management Tracks (Rule 1.200)

### Streamlined Track
- Simplified cases
- Limited discovery
- Expedited resolution
- 180-day target

### General Track
- Standard civil cases
- Full discovery allowed
- 12-18 month target

### Complex Track
- Multi-party litigation
- Extensive discovery
- Dedicated judge assignment
- 24+ month target
