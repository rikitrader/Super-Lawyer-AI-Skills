## Expert Practice Framework

```
EXPERT PIPELINE:
1. Retain Expert → Engagement letter; materials provided
2. Disclose Expert → Per case management order
3. Depose Expert → Within discovery period
4. Challenge Expert → Daubert motion
5. Present Expert → Direct/cross examination
```

---

## 10.1 Expert Disclosure

### PLAINTIFF'S EXPERT WITNESS DISCLOSURE
**PURPOSE:** Identify retained experts and opinions
**WHEN TO USE:** Per case management order deadline
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Required for expert testimony; locks in opinions
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Expert identity; qualifications; opinions; bases; compensation
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.280(a)(5)

### DEFENDANT'S EXPERT WITNESS DISCLOSURE
**PURPOSE:** Identify defense experts and opinions
**WHEN TO USE:** Per case management order (typically after plaintiff's)
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Rebuttal experts; defense theories
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Same as plaintiff's disclosure
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.280(a)(5)

### SUPPLEMENTAL EXPERT WITNESS DISCLOSURE
**PURPOSE:** Update expert disclosure with new information
**WHEN TO USE:** When opinions change; additional bases identified
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Preserve ability to offer testimony
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Changes to prior disclosure
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.280(e)

### REBUTTAL EXPERT WITNESS DISCLOSURE
**PURPOSE:** Identify experts to rebut opponent's experts
**WHEN TO USE:** After opponent's expert disclosure; per CMO
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Counter opposing expert opinions
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Expert identity; specific rebuttal opinions
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.280(a)(5)

## 10.2 Expert Depositions

### NOTICE OF TAKING EXPERT DEPOSITION
**PURPOSE:** Schedule deposition of expert witness
**WHEN TO USE:** After expert disclosure; within discovery period
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Test opinions; prepare cross; identify weaknesses
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Reasonable notice; location (often expert's office)
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310

### NOTICE OF TAKING EXPERT DEPOSITION DUCES TECUM
**PURPOSE:** Deposition with document production
**WHEN TO USE:** When expert's file and materials needed
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Review all materials expert considered
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Document categories; file review
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(b)(5)

### MOTION TO COMPEL EXPERT DEPOSITION
**PURPOSE:** Force expert to appear for deposition
**WHEN TO USE:** Expert refusing or delaying
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Complete discovery; potential sanctions
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Attempts to schedule; refusal; relevance
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.380(a)

### MOTION FOR PROTECTIVE ORDER RE: EXPERT DEPOSITION
**PURPOSE:** Limit scope or manner of expert deposition
**WHEN TO USE:** Harassment; proprietary methods; repetitive questions
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Protect expert; limit discovery
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific concerns; proposed limits
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.280(c)

## 10.3 Daubert Challenges

### MOTION TO EXCLUDE EXPERT TESTIMONY (DAUBERT)
**PURPOSE:** Challenge expert under reliability standards
**WHEN TO USE:** Pretrial; challenge methodology or qualifications
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Eliminate opponent's expert; potentially dispositive
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Daubert factors; methodology critique; qualifications issues
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 90.702

### MEMORANDUM IN SUPPORT OF DAUBERT MOTION
**PURPOSE:** Legal argument for expert exclusion
**WHEN TO USE:** With Daubert motion
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Detailed reliability analysis
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Factor-by-factor analysis; case law
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 90.702

### RESPONSE IN OPPOSITION TO DAUBERT MOTION
**PURPOSE:** Defend expert against Daubert challenge
**WHEN TO USE:** When opponent challenges your expert
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Preserve expert testimony
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Reliability showing; factor analysis; case law
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 90.702

### MOTION FOR DAUBERT HEARING
**PURPOSE:** Request evidentiary hearing on expert qualifications
**WHEN TO USE:** When reliability disputed; court needs live testimony
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Test expert before trial
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Request for hearing; issues to be resolved
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 90.702

### REPLY IN SUPPORT OF DAUBERT MOTION
**PURPOSE:** Address opponent's response to Daubert challenge
**WHEN TO USE:** After opposition filed
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Final word on reliability issues
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Response to opposition arguments
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 90.702

## 10.4 Expert-Related Discovery

### INTERROGATORIES RE: EXPERT WITNESSES
**PURPOSE:** Written questions about opponent's experts
**WHEN TO USE:** Early discovery; identify experts
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Discover expert identities and opinions
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Within 30-interrogatory limit
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.340

### REQUEST FOR PRODUCTION OF EXPERT FILE
**PURPOSE:** Obtain all materials provided to/created by expert
**WHEN TO USE:** After expert disclosure
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Review everything expert considered
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific categories; work product objections may apply
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.350

### MOTION TO COMPEL PRODUCTION OF EXPERT FILE
**PURPOSE:** Force production of expert's complete file
**WHEN TO USE:** Incomplete production; materials withheld
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Complete expert record
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Deficiencies; relevance; scope of disclosure rule
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.380(a)

## 10.5 Expert Witness Fees

### MOTION FOR EXPERT WITNESS FEES
**PURPOSE:** Recover expert costs as prevailing party
**WHEN TO USE:** Post-judgment; if contract/statute provides
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Additional recovery
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Fee basis; invoices; reasonableness
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 57.071

### OBJECTION TO EXPERT WITNESS FEES
**PURPOSE:** Challenge expert fee recovery
**WHEN TO USE:** When opponent seeks excessive fees
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Reduce fee liability
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Reasonableness challenge; necessity
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 57.071

## 10.6 Motions in Limine - Experts

### MOTION IN LIMINE TO EXCLUDE UNDISCLOSED EXPERT OPINIONS
**PURPOSE:** Bar opinions not in disclosure
**WHEN TO USE:** Expert seeks to offer new opinions at trial
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Limit testimony to disclosed opinions
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Disclosure requirements; surprise/prejudice
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.280(a)(5)

### MOTION IN LIMINE TO EXCLUDE EXPERT ON [SPECIFIC TOPIC]
**PURPOSE:** Bar expert from specific subject matter
**WHEN TO USE:** Expert straying beyond expertise
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Limit scope of testimony
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Qualifications analysis; scope of expertise
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 90.702

### MOTION IN LIMINE TO EXCLUDE EXPERT'S LEGAL CONCLUSIONS
**PURPOSE:** Bar expert from opining on law
**WHEN TO USE:** Expert offering legal opinions
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Prevent invasion of court's role
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Legal conclusion identification
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 90.702

## Daubert Factors (Fla. Stat. § 90.702)

### Reliability Analysis

| Factor | Question | Evidence |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Testing | Can the theory be tested? | Testing methodology |
| Peer Review | Has it been published/reviewed? | Publications; acceptance |
| Error Rate | What is the known error rate? | Studies; statistics |
| Standards | Are there controlling standards? | Guidelines; protocols |
| Acceptance | Is it generally accepted? | Field consensus |

### Qualification Requirements

Expert must be qualified by:
- Knowledge
- Skill
- Experience
- Training
- Education

### Testimony Requirements

1. Based on sufficient facts or data
2. Product of reliable principles and methods
3. Expert applied principles reliably to the facts
