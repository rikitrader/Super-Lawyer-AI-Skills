## Appellate Strategic Framework

```
APPEAL PIPELINE:
1. Preserve Error → Object at trial; make record
2. Notice of Appeal → 30 days from rendition
3. Record on Appeal → Designate; prepare
4. Briefs → Initial, Answer, Reply
5. Oral Argument → If granted
6. Decision → Affirm, Reverse, Remand
```

## 14.1 Notice of Appeal

### NOTICE OF APPEAL
**PURPOSE:** Initiate appeal to District Court of Appeal
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 30 days of rendition of final order
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Jurisdiction over appeal; extends trial court jurisdiction
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Identification of order appealed; parties; court
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.110(b)

### AMENDED NOTICE OF APPEAL
**PURPOSE:** Correct or supplement notice of appeal
**WHEN TO USE:** Error in original notice; add orders
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Ensure proper scope of appeal
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Corrected information; within time limits
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.110(b)

### CROSS-APPEAL NOTICE
**PURPOSE:** Appeal by appellee from adverse portions
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 10 days of notice of appeal; or with answer brief
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Challenge unfavorable rulings while defending
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Identification of adverse rulings
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.110(g)

### NOTICE OF APPEAL FROM NON-FINAL ORDER
**PURPOSE:** Appeal interlocutory order
**WHEN TO USE:** Order falls within Fla. R. App. P. 9.130(a)(3)
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Challenge order before final judgment
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specify order; show appealability
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.130

## 14.2 Record on Appeal

### DESIGNATION TO COURT REPORTER
**PURPOSE:** Request preparation of transcript
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 10 days of notice of appeal
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Include relevant proceedings in record
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Identify proceedings; estimate pages
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.200(b)

### DIRECTIONS TO CLERK
**PURPOSE:** Specify record contents
**WHEN TO USE:** With designation to reporter
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Ensure complete record; include exhibits
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** List of documents/exhibits to include
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.200(a)

### MOTION TO SUPPLEMENT RECORD
**PURPOSE:** Add items to appellate record
**WHEN TO USE:** Record incomplete; important items missing
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Ensure reviewing court has all needed materials
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Identification of items; relevance
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.200(f)

### MOTION TO CORRECT RECORD
**PURPOSE:** Fix errors in appellate record
**WHEN TO USE:** Record inaccurate or incomplete
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Accurate record for review
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific errors; proposed corrections
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.200(f)

## 14.3 Stays Pending Appeal

### MOTION TO STAY PENDING APPEAL
**PURPOSE:** Halt enforcement of judgment during appeal
**WHEN TO USE:** After notice of appeal; to preserve status quo
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Prevent irreparable harm during appeal
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Likelihood of success; bond if monetary judgment
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.310

### MOTION FOR SUPERSEDEAS BOND
**PURPOSE:** Stay money judgment pending appeal
**WHEN TO USE:** Money judgment entered; want to stay execution
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Prevent collection during appeal
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Bond in amount of judgment plus interest/costs
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.310(b)

### MOTION TO VACATE STAY
**PURPOSE:** Remove stay pending appeal
**WHEN TO USE:** Appellee wants enforcement during appeal
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Allow immediate enforcement
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Why stay inappropriate; bond inadequate
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.310

## 14.4 Extensions and Procedural

### MOTION FOR EXTENSION OF TIME (BRIEFS/RECORD)
**PURPOSE:** Extend deadline for appellate filings
**WHEN TO USE:** Good cause; unable to meet deadline
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Additional time to prepare
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Good cause; proposed new deadline
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.300(a)

### MOTION TO CONSOLIDATE APPEALS
**PURPOSE:** Combine related appeals
**WHEN TO USE:** Same or related parties; common issues
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Judicial economy; consistent rulings
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Common issues; no prejudice
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.300

### MOTION TO EXPEDITE APPEAL
**PURPOSE:** Accelerate appellate schedule
**WHEN TO USE:** Urgency; matter requires quick resolution
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Faster decision
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Good cause; urgency
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.300

### MOTION TO DISMISS APPEAL
**PURPOSE:** Terminate appeal
**WHEN TO USE:** Jurisdictional defect; mootness; abandonment
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** End appeal without merits decision
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Basis for dismissal
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.350

### MOTION TO ACCEPT LATE FILING
**PURPOSE:** Accept untimely brief or filing
**WHEN TO USE:** Deadline missed; good cause
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Avoid default/dismissal
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Good cause; no prejudice
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.300(a)

## 14.5 Briefs

### INITIAL BRIEF (APPELLANT)
**PURPOSE:** Present arguments for reversal
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 70 days of notice of appeal
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Primary advocacy document
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Statement of case; issues; argument; conclusion
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.210(b)

### ANSWER BRIEF (APPELLEE)
**PURPOSE:** Respond to appellant's arguments
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 20 days of initial brief
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Defend judgment; respond to errors
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Response to issues; counter-argument
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.210(c)

### REPLY BRIEF (APPELLANT)
**PURPOSE:** Address new matters in answer brief
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 20 days of answer brief
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Final word; address appellee's arguments
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Limited to reply matters
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.210(d)

### CROSS-APPELLANT'S BRIEF
**PURPOSE:** Present cross-appeal arguments
**WHEN TO USE:** When cross-appeal filed
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Seek reversal on additional grounds
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Issues on cross-appeal; argument
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.210

### AMICUS CURIAE BRIEF
**PURPOSE:** Present non-party's perspective
**WHEN TO USE:** When court grants leave or rules allow
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Additional support for position
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Motion for leave; interest in case
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.370

## 14.6 Post-Decision

### MOTION FOR REHEARING
**PURPOSE:** Request reconsideration of decision
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 15 days of decision
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Correct errors; clarify opinion
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific grounds; what court overlooked
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.330

### MOTION FOR REHEARING EN BANC
**PURPOSE:** Request full court reconsideration
**WHEN TO USE:** With motion for rehearing; exceptional case
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Full court review; resolve intra-court conflict
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Exceptional importance; conflict with prior decisions
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.331

### MOTION FOR CERTIFICATION
**PURPOSE:** Request certification of question or conflict
**WHEN TO USE:** Issue of great public importance; DCA conflict
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Path to Florida Supreme Court review
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Importance; conflict; certified question
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.330(a)

### MOTION FOR CLARIFICATION
**PURPOSE:** Clarify ambiguous decision
**WHEN TO USE:** Decision unclear; mandate uncertain
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Clear guidance for trial court
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific ambiguity; requested clarification
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.330

### NOTICE TO INVOKE DISCRETIONARY JURISDICTION (FLORIDA SUPREME COURT)
**PURPOSE:** Seek Florida Supreme Court review
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 30 days of DCA decision; constitutional issue or conflict
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Highest state court review
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Jurisdictional basis; Art. V, § 3(b) grounds
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. App. P. 9.120

## 14.7 Standards of Review

### Summary: Standards of Review by Issue Type

| Issue Type | Standard | Deference |
|------------|----------|-----------|
| Questions of law | De novo | None |
| Constitutional issues | De novo | None |
| Jury instructions | De novo | None |
| Summary judgment | De novo | None |
| Factual findings (bench) | Competent substantial evidence | High |
| Credibility determinations | Substantial evidence | Very high |
| Discretionary rulings | Abuse of discretion | High |
| Discovery rulings | Abuse of discretion | High |
| Evidentiary rulings | Abuse of discretion | High |
| New trial motions | Abuse of discretion | High |
| Sanctions | Abuse of discretion | High |
| Mixed questions | De novo (law) / CSE (fact) | Split |

## Appellate Checklist

### After Adverse Judgment
- [ ] Identify appealable issues
- [ ] Check preservation (objections made)
- [ ] Calculate 30-day deadline
- [ ] Assess likelihood of success
- [ ] Consider cost/benefit

### Filing Appeal
- [ ] File notice of appeal (30 days)
- [ ] Pay filing fee
- [ ] Designate record (10 days)
- [ ] Order transcript
- [ ] Consider stay/supersedeas

### Briefing
- [ ] Initial brief (70 days)
- [ ] Follow format rules (Fla. R. App. P. 9.210)
- [ ] Include appendix
- [ ] Serve opposing counsel
- [ ] File with court

### Post-Decision
- [ ] Review for rehearing issues (15 days)
- [ ] Consider Supreme Court review (30 days)
- [ ] Monitor mandate
- [ ] Comply with remand
