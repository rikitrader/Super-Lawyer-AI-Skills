## Trial Motions

### MOTION FOR DIRECTED VERDICT
**PURPOSE:** Obtain verdict as matter of law during trial
**WHEN TO USE:** After plaintiff rests; after all evidence
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Win without jury deliberation
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** No reasonable jury could find for opponent
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.480

### MOTION FOR MISTRIAL
**PURPOSE:** Declare trial void due to prejudicial error
**WHEN TO USE:** Incurable prejudice occurs
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Start over; preserve error
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific prejudicial conduct; no cure possible
**RULE REFERENCE:** Court's inherent authority

### MOTION TO REOPEN EVIDENCE
**PURPOSE:** Allow additional evidence after close
**WHEN TO USE:** Newly discovered evidence; oversight
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Complete the record
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Good cause; materiality
**RULE REFERENCE:** Court's discretion

### MOTION TO EXCLUDE WITNESSES (RULE OF SEQUESTRATION)
**PURPOSE:** Prevent witnesses from hearing other testimony
**WHEN TO USE:** At trial start; prevent tailoring testimony
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** More honest testimony
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Request invoking rule
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 90.616

### MOTION FOR ADDITIONAL PEREMPTORY CHALLENGES
**PURPOSE:** Obtain more strikes in jury selection
**WHEN TO USE:** Complex case; multiple parties
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Better jury composition
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Complexity; number of parties
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.431

### MOTION FOR CAUSE CHALLENGE
**PURPOSE:** Strike juror for bias
**WHEN TO USE:** During voir dire
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Remove biased juror without using peremptory
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific bias shown
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.431(c)

### MOTION TO STRIKE JUROR FOR CAUSE
**PURPOSE:** Remove biased juror
**WHEN TO USE:** Bias revealed in voir dire
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Protect fair trial
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Bias; inability to be fair
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.431(c)

### BATSON CHALLENGE
**PURPOSE:** Challenge discriminatory jury selection
**WHEN TO USE:** Pattern of strikes against protected class
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Prevent discrimination; potentially seat struck jurors
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Pattern; prima facie case
**RULE REFERENCE:** Batson v. Kentucky; Fla. Stat. § 40.01

### MOTION TO POLL THE JURY
**PURPOSE:** Confirm individual juror agreement with verdict
**WHEN TO USE:** After verdict announced
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Ensure unanimity; preserve record
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Request before jury dismissed
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.481

### MOTION FOR ADDITUR OR REMITTITUR
**PURPOSE:** Adjust damage award
**WHEN TO USE:** Verdict inadequate (additur) or excessive (remittitur)
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Correct disproportionate awards
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Evidence of proper damages; verdict analysis
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.530(a)

## Trial Documents

### WITNESS LIST (FINAL)
**PURPOSE:** Final list of trial witnesses
**WHEN TO USE:** Per pretrial order; before trial
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Lock in witnesses; prevent additions
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Name; expected testimony summary
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.200

### EXHIBIT LIST (FINAL)
**PURPOSE:** Final list of trial exhibits
**WHEN TO USE:** Per pretrial order; before trial
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Lock in exhibits; prevent additions
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Exhibit number; description; witness
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.200

### PROPOSED JURY INSTRUCTIONS (FINAL)
**PURPOSE:** Requested jury charges
**WHEN TO USE:** Per pretrial order; before charge conference
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Shape law given to jury
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Standard instructions; special instructions with authority
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.470

### VERDICT FORM (FINAL)
**PURPOSE:** Questions for jury to answer
**WHEN TO USE:** With jury instructions
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Frame jury's decision
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Clear questions; elements covered
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.480

### TRIAL BRIEF
**PURPOSE:** Legal memorandum for trial issues
**WHEN TO USE:** Before trial; for judge reference
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Educate judge on key issues
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Legal argument; citation to record
**RULE REFERENCE:** Local practice

### OPENING STATEMENT OUTLINE
**PURPOSE:** Framework for opening statement
**WHEN TO USE:** Trial preparation
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Effective trial advocacy
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Key facts; evidence preview
**RULE REFERENCE:** Advocacy practice

### CLOSING ARGUMENT OUTLINE
**PURPOSE:** Framework for closing argument
**WHEN TO USE:** Trial preparation; during trial
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Persuasive summation
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Evidence summary; law application
**RULE REFERENCE:** Advocacy practice

## Jury Selection Documents

### JURY QUESTIONNAIRE
**PURPOSE:** Written questions for prospective jurors
**WHEN TO USE:** Before voir dire; if court permits
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Pre-screening; targeted follow-up
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Proposed questions; court approval
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.431

### JURY SEATING CHART
**PURPOSE:** Track juror positions and notes
**WHEN TO USE:** During voir dire
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Organized selection process
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Panel layout; note system
**RULE REFERENCE:** Advocacy practice

### STRIKE LIST
**PURPOSE:** Track challenges used
**WHEN TO USE:** During jury selection
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Manage limited strikes
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Cause/peremptory tracking
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.431

## Trial Checklist

### Pre-Trial
- [ ] Witness subpoenas served
- [ ] Exhibits organized and marked
- [ ] Technology tested
- [ ] Opening statement prepared
- [ ] Direct examinations outlined
- [ ] Cross-examinations outlined
- [ ] Jury instructions finalized

### During Trial
- [ ] Object when appropriate (preserve record)
- [ ] Move to strike if answer before ruling
- [ ] Track admitted exhibits
- [ ] Monitor jury reactions
- [ ] Renew directed verdict motion

### Post-Verdict
- [ ] Poll jury if appropriate
- [ ] Note deadline for post-trial motions (15 days)
- [ ] Consider JNOV, new trial, remittitur/additur
