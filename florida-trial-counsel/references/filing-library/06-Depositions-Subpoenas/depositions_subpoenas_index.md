## 6.1 Deposition Notices

### NOTICE OF TAKING DEPOSITION
**PURPOSE:** Schedule oral deposition of witness
**WHEN TO USE:** After discovery opens; need live testimony
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Lock in testimony; assess witness; impeachment potential
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Reasonable notice; time/place; recording method
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(b)

### NOTICE OF TAKING DEPOSITION DUCES TECUM
**PURPOSE:** Deposition with document production requirement
**WHEN TO USE:** Witness has relevant documents
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Obtain documents and testimony simultaneously
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Description of documents; reasonable notice
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(b)(5)

### NOTICE OF TAKING VIDEOTAPED DEPOSITION
**PURPOSE:** Record deposition on video
**WHEN TO USE:** Preserve witness demeanor; potential trial use
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** More impactful at trial; show witness manner
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Notice must specify video recording
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(b)(4)

### NOTICE OF TAKING DEPOSITION BY TELEPHONE
**PURPOSE:** Conduct deposition remotely
**WHEN TO USE:** Witness distant; convenience; cost savings
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Reduced travel costs; broader witness access
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specify telephonic method; arrangements for exhibits
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(b)(7)

### NOTICE OF TAKING DEPOSITION BY ZOOM/REMOTE MEANS
**PURPOSE:** Conduct deposition via video conference
**WHEN TO USE:** Modern practice; pandemic protocols; convenience
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Face-to-face without travel; exhibit sharing
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Technology arrangements; notice of platform
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(b)(7)

### AMENDED NOTICE OF TAKING DEPOSITION
**PURPOSE:** Change deposition date/time/location
**WHEN TO USE:** Original date doesn't work; scheduling conflict
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Reschedule without starting over
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** New date/time/location; reasonable notice
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(b)

### NOTICE OF TAKING CORPORATE REPRESENTATIVE DEPOSITION (30(b)(6) STYLE)
**PURPOSE:** Depose organization through designated representative
**WHEN TO USE:** Need organizational testimony; multiple possible witnesses
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Organization bound by representative's testimony
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Topic list for designation; reasonable notice
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(b)(6)

### NOTICE OF FILING DEPOSITION TRANSCRIPT
**PURPOSE:** File deposition with court
**WHEN TO USE:** For use in motions; trial; or court requirement
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Official court record
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Certified transcript
**RULE REFERENCE:** Local rules

## 6.2 Subpoenas

### SUBPOENA FOR DEPOSITION
**PURPOSE:** Compel witness attendance at deposition
**WHEN TO USE:** Non-party witness; or to compel attendance
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Enforceable attendance; contempt for failure
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Proper form; service; witness fees tendered
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.410

### SUBPOENA DUCES TECUM TO NON-PARTY
**PURPOSE:** Compel non-party document production
**WHEN TO USE:** Third party has relevant documents
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Obtain independent evidence
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific documents described; notice to parties
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.351

### SUBPOENA FOR TRIAL
**PURPOSE:** Compel witness attendance at trial
**WHEN TO USE:** Ensure witness appears at trial
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Enforceable attendance; contempt for failure
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Proper form; service; witness fees
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.410

### SUBPOENA DUCES TECUM FOR TRIAL
**PURPOSE:** Compel witness with documents at trial
**WHEN TO USE:** Witness needed with specific documents
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Documents and testimony at trial
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Document description; proper service
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.410

### NOTICE OF INTENT TO SERVE SUBPOENA ON NON-PARTY
**PURPOSE:** Notify parties before serving non-party subpoena
**WHEN TO USE:** Required before non-party document subpoena
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Opportunity for parties to object
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Copy of proposed subpoena; 10 days notice
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.351

## 6.3 Deposition Motions

### MOTION TO QUASH DEPOSITION NOTICE
**PURPOSE:** Cancel improperly noticed deposition
**WHEN TO USE:** Notice defective; timing improper; harassment
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Block improper deposition
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific deficiencies; prejudice
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(d)

### MOTION TO QUASH SUBPOENA
**PURPOSE:** Cancel improperly issued subpoena
**WHEN TO USE:** Subpoena improper; burdensome; privileged matter
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Block improper discovery
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific grounds; privilege if applicable
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.410

### OBJECTIONS TO SUBPOENA DUCES TECUM
**PURPOSE:** Challenge document subpoena
**WHEN TO USE:** Subpoena overbroad; privileged; burdensome
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Limit production obligations
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific objections to each request
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.351

### MOTION TO COMPEL NON-PARTY COMPLIANCE WITH SUBPOENA
**PURPOSE:** Force non-party to comply with subpoena
**WHEN TO USE:** Non-party refuses to comply
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Obtain evidence from reluctant witness
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Subpoena served; non-compliance; relevance
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.351

### MOTION FOR PROTECTIVE ORDER RE: DEPOSITION TOPICS
**PURPOSE:** Limit deposition scope
**WHEN TO USE:** Topics privileged, harassing, or irrelevant
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Protect witness; limit inquiry
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific topics challenged; legal basis
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.280(c)

### MOTION TO COMPEL CORPORATE REPRESENTATIVE DEPOSITION
**PURPOSE:** Force organization to designate witness
**WHEN TO USE:** Organization refuses to designate; inadequate witness
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Obtain organizational testimony
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Topics noticed; failure to designate
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(b)(6)

### MOTION TO LIMIT DEPOSITION
**PURPOSE:** Restrict duration or scope of deposition
**WHEN TO USE:** Standard limits exceeded; harassment
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Prevent deposition abuse
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Basis for limit; what's already covered
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(d)

### MOTION TO REOPEN DEPOSITION
**PURPOSE:** Take additional deposition testimony
**WHEN TO USE:** New documents discovered; incomplete testimony
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Complete the record
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Good cause; limited scope
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310

### MOTION FOR SANCTIONS FOR DEPOSITION MISCONDUCT
**PURPOSE:** Sanction improper conduct during deposition
**WHEN TO USE:** Witness coached; objections obstructive; bad faith
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Deter misconduct; recover costs
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific misconduct; transcript excerpts
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.380

## 6.4 Deposition Procedure Documents

### STIPULATION REGARDING DEPOSITION PROCEDURES
**PURPOSE:** Agree on deposition logistics
**WHEN TO USE:** Modify standard procedures; remote depositions
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Streamline process; avoid disputes
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Agreement on procedures; both signatures
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310

### DEPOSITION ERRATA SHEET
**PURPOSE:** Correct transcript errors
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 30 days of receiving transcript
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Fix errors; but changes noted
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific corrections; reasons for each
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310(e)

### CERTIFICATE OF NON-APPEARANCE
**PURPOSE:** Document witness failure to appear
**WHEN TO USE:** Witness fails to appear at noticed deposition
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Basis for sanctions; costs
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Reporter's certificate; notice documentation
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.310

## Deposition Checklist

### Before Deposition
- [ ] Notice properly served (reasonable time)
- [ ] Subpoena served if non-party
- [ ] Witness fees tendered
- [ ] Deposition outline prepared
- [ ] Exhibits organized
- [ ] Reporter/videographer arranged
- [ ] Location confirmed

### During Deposition
- [ ] Proper oath administered
- [ ] Objections stated for record (form only at deposition)
- [ ] Exhibits marked
- [ ] Document production tracked
- [ ] Key testimony obtained
- [ ] Impeachment material preserved

### After Deposition
- [ ] Transcript ordered
- [ ] Errata deadline noted (30 days)
- [ ] Key excerpts identified
- [ ] Filing deadline if needed
