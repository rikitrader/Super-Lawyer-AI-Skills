## Post-Judgment Strategic Framework

```
ENFORCEMENT PIPELINE:
1. Record Judgment → Create lien
2. Discover Assets → Proceedings supplementary
3. Execute → Garnishment, attachment, levy
4. Collect → Satisfaction of judgment
```

---

## 13.1 Post-Trial Motions

### MOTION FOR NEW TRIAL
**PURPOSE:** Request trial be conducted again
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 15 days of judgment; error requires new trial
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Second chance; but high standard
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Grounds under Rule 1.530; prejudicial error
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.530

### MOTION FOR JUDGMENT NOTWITHSTANDING THE VERDICT (JNOV)
**PURPOSE:** Reverse jury verdict as matter of law
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 15 days; verdict unsupported by evidence
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Overturn unfavorable verdict
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** No reasonable jury could find as it did
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.480(b)

### RENEWED MOTION FOR DIRECTED VERDICT
**PURPOSE:** Renew pre-verdict motion for judgment as matter of law
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 15 days; after adverse verdict
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Preserve issue for appeal; potentially reverse
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Prior directed verdict motion; insufficient evidence
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.480(b)

### MOTION TO AMEND JUDGMENT
**PURPOSE:** Correct clerical errors or omissions in judgment
**WHEN TO USE:** Within jurisdiction; clerical correction needed
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Fix mistakes; clarify terms
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific error; proposed correction
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.540(a)

### MOTION FOR REMITTITUR
**PURPOSE:** Reduce excessive damage award
**WHEN TO USE:** Verdict shock conscience; no evidence supports amount
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Reduce plaintiff's recovery
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Award analysis; comparison to evidence
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.530

### MOTION FOR ADDITUR
**PURPOSE:** Increase inadequate damage award
**WHEN TO USE:** Verdict grossly inadequate
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Increase recovery
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Evidence of higher damages; inadequacy analysis
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.530

### MOTION TO ALTER OR AMEND JUDGMENT
**PURPOSE:** Request substantive change to judgment
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 15 days; to correct errors or add findings
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Modify unfavorable ruling
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Basis for alteration; manifest error
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.530(g)

## 13.2 Relief from Judgment

### MOTION TO VACATE FINAL JUDGMENT
**PURPOSE:** Set aside judgment
**WHEN TO USE:** Fraud, mistake, newly discovered evidence, void judgment
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Second chance; nullify adverse judgment
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Rule 1.540(b) grounds; timeliness
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.540(b)

### MOTION FOR RELIEF FROM JUDGMENT (RULE 1.540)
**PURPOSE:** Comprehensive relief from judgment
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 1 year (most grounds); any time if void
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Various bases for relief
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific ground; supporting evidence
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.540(b)

### MOTION TO SET ASIDE DEFAULT JUDGMENT
**PURPOSE:** Vacate judgment entered by default
**WHEN TO USE:** Excusable neglect; meritorious defense
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Restore right to defend
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Reason for default; meritorious defense; diligence
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.540(b)

## 13.3 Costs and Attorney's Fees

### MOTION TO TAX COSTS
**PURPOSE:** Obtain costs from losing party
**WHEN TO USE:** Within 30 days of judgment; prevailing party
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Recover litigation expenses
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Bill of costs; supporting receipts
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.420; Fla. Stat. § 57.041

### MOTION FOR ATTORNEY'S FEES
**PURPOSE:** Recover attorney's fees
**WHEN TO USE:** Contract or statute provides; prevailing party
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Major additional recovery
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Fee basis (contract/statute); time records; reasonableness
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 57.105; Contract terms

### AFFIDAVIT OF ATTORNEY'S FEES
**PURPOSE:** Support fee request with detailed records
**WHEN TO USE:** With fee motion
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Establish reasonable fees
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Time entries; hourly rate; reasonableness factors
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 57.105

### MOTION FOR PREVAILING PARTY COSTS
**PURPOSE:** Establish prevailing party for costs
**WHEN TO USE:** When prevailing party status disputed
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Prerequisite to cost award
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Analysis of who prevailed
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.420

### OBJECTION TO BILL OF COSTS
**PURPOSE:** Challenge claimed costs
**WHEN TO USE:** Within time allowed; costs improper
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Reduce cost liability
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Specific objections; unallowable items
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 57.041

### MOTION TO REDUCE ATTORNEY'S FEES
**PURPOSE:** Challenge excessive fee request
**WHEN TO USE:** Fee request unreasonable
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Reduce fee liability
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Reasonableness analysis; comparable rates
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 57.105

## 13.4 Enforcement - Writs and Execution

### MOTION FOR WRIT OF EXECUTION
**PURPOSE:** Obtain levy on judgment debtor's property
**WHEN TO USE:** Judgment final; property to execute against
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Seize and sell property to satisfy judgment
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Certified judgment; property identified
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 56.021

### WRIT OF EXECUTION
**PURPOSE:** Court order directing sheriff to levy
**WHEN TO USE:** After motion granted
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Actual property seizure
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Signed by clerk; delivered to sheriff
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 56.021

### MOTION FOR WRIT OF GARNISHMENT
**PURPOSE:** Reach debtor's property held by third party
**WHEN TO USE:** Debtor has funds in bank; wages; accounts receivable
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Access debtor's assets through others
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Motion; affidavit; bond (if pre-judgment)
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 77.01

### WRIT OF GARNISHMENT
**PURPOSE:** Order third party to hold debtor's property
**WHEN TO USE:** After motion granted
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Freeze and collect funds
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Proper form; service on garnishee
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 77.03

### MOTION FOR WRIT OF ATTACHMENT
**PURPOSE:** Seize debtor's property pre-judgment
**WHEN TO USE:** Fraud, removal, or disposal of assets threatened
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Preserve assets before judgment
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Statutory grounds; bond required
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 76.01

### WRIT OF ATTACHMENT
**PURPOSE:** Order to seize debtor's property pre-judgment
**WHEN TO USE:** After motion granted
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Property held pending judgment
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Bond posted; proper form
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 76.05

### MOTION FOR WRIT OF POSSESSION
**PURPOSE:** Obtain possession of specific property
**WHEN TO USE:** Replevin judgment; landlord-tenant; foreclosure
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Actual possession of property
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Judgment for possession; bond if pre-judgment
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 78.01

### WRIT OF POSSESSION
**PURPOSE:** Order to deliver possession
**WHEN TO USE:** After motion/judgment
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Sheriff removes occupants; delivers property
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Proper form; sheriff execution
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 78.09

## 13.5 Proceedings Supplementary

### MOTION FOR PROCEEDINGS SUPPLEMENTARY
**PURPOSE:** Discover debtor's assets; reach property
**WHEN TO USE:** Execution returned unsatisfied; assets unknown
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Asset discovery; implead third parties
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Unsatisfied execution; need for discovery
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 56.29

### NOTICE OF PROCEEDINGS SUPPLEMENTARY
**PURPOSE:** Initiate asset discovery process
**WHEN TO USE:** After motion granted
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Compel debtor appearance; asset disclosure
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Court order; notice to debtor
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 56.29

### SUBPOENA FOR DEBTOR EXAMINATION
**PURPOSE:** Compel debtor to testify about assets
**WHEN TO USE:** In proceedings supplementary
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Discover hidden assets; bank accounts
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Subpoena; questions prepared
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 56.29

### MOTION TO IMPLEAD THIRD PARTY (PROCEEDINGS SUPPLEMENTARY)
**PURPOSE:** Bring in person holding debtor's assets
**WHEN TO USE:** Third party has debtor's property
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Reach assets held by others
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Evidence third party holds assets
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 56.29

### MOTION AND ORDER TO SHOW CAUSE
**PURPOSE:** Compel debtor to explain non-payment
**WHEN TO USE:** Debtor has ability to pay but refuses
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Contempt possible; pressure payment
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Evidence of ability to pay; non-payment
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 56.29

## 13.6 Contempt and Enforcement

### MOTION FOR CONTEMPT / ENFORCEMENT
**PURPOSE:** Enforce court orders through contempt
**WHEN TO USE:** Party violates order; refuses compliance
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Jail, fines, coerced compliance
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Clear order; willful violation; ability to comply
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.570

### MOTION FOR INDIRECT CRIMINAL CONTEMPT
**PURPOSE:** Punish willful violation of court order
**WHEN TO USE:** Serious violation; punitive sanction needed
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Criminal penalties; incarceration possible
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Order violated; willful violation; proper procedure
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Crim. P. 3.840

### MOTION FOR CIVIL CONTEMPT
**PURPOSE:** Coerce compliance with court order
**WHEN TO USE:** Ongoing violation; compliance possible
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Incarceration until compliance; "keys to cell"
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Clear order; violation; ability to comply
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.570

## 13.7 Satisfaction and Release

### SATISFACTION OF JUDGMENT
**PURPOSE:** Document judgment paid in full
**WHEN TO USE:** Judgment satisfied; required by law
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Clear debtor's record; release lien
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Payment received; proper form
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 55.141

### PARTIAL SATISFACTION OF JUDGMENT
**PURPOSE:** Document partial payment
**WHEN TO USE:** Partial payment received
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Credit against judgment; reduce balance
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Payment received; remaining balance
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 55.141

### MOTION TO COMPEL SATISFACTION OF JUDGMENT
**PURPOSE:** Force creditor to file satisfaction
**WHEN TO USE:** Judgment paid but satisfaction not filed
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Clear record; penalties for non-filing
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Evidence of payment; no satisfaction filed
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 55.141

### RELEASE OF LIEN
**PURPOSE:** Release judgment lien from property
**WHEN TO USE:** Judgment satisfied; property sold
**STRATEGIC IMPACT:** Clear title
**REQUIRED SUPPORT:** Payment/satisfaction; proper form
**RULE REFERENCE:** Fla. Stat. § 55.10

## Post-Judgment Checklist

### Judgment Obtained
- [ ] Record judgment (creates lien)
- [ ] Assess collectibility
- [ ] Identify assets
- [ ] Choose enforcement method

### Enforcement
- [ ] Writ of execution if property known
- [ ] Garnishment if bank/wages known
- [ ] Proceedings supplementary if assets hidden
- [ ] Regular asset checks
- [ ] Monitor for new assets

### After Collection
- [ ] File satisfaction of judgment
- [ ] Release liens
- [ ] Close file
