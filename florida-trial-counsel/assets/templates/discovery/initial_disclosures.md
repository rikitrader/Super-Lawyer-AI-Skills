---

## Document Information

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Purpose** | Mandatory initial disclosures under amended Rule 1.280(a) |
| **When to Use** | Within 60 days after service of complaint |
| **Filing Notes** | Serve on all parties; do not file with court unless ordered |
| **Rule Reference** | Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.280(a) (effective January 1, 2025) |

## Completion Checklist

- [ ] Deadline: 60 days after service of complaint
- [ ] List all witnesses who may support claims/defenses
- [ ] Include full contact information for each witness
- [ ] Describe subject matter of each witness's knowledge
- [ ] Attach or describe all supporting documents
- [ ] Provide damages computation with specificity
- [ ] Include supporting documents for damages
- [ ] Identify all potentially applicable insurance
- [ ] Attach insurance policies or declarations pages
- [ ] Serve on all parties (do not file with court)
- [ ] Calendar supplementation duty

---

## Key Rule Requirements (Rule 1.280(a))

**Timing:**
- Due within 60 days after service of the complaint
- Parties may agree in writing to extend (up to 30 additional days without court order)

**Scope:**
- Information the disclosing party **may use to support its claims or defenses**
- Not required to disclose unfavorable information (but may be discoverable)

**Form:**
- Documents: Provide copies OR describe by category and location
- Damages: Provide computation AND documents supporting calculation
- Insurance: Provide copy of policy for inspection and copying

**Supplementation (Rule 1.280(e)):**
- Continuing duty to supplement if information is incomplete or incorrect
- Must supplement in a timely manner

## Risk Flags

**ATTORNEY REVIEW REQUIRED:**
- [ ] Verify all witness contact information is current
- [ ] Confirm damages calculation is accurate and supportable
- [ ] Review insurance policies for coverage applicability
- [ ] Consider privilege issues before producing documents
- [ ] Evaluate work product protection for certain materials
- [ ] Ensure no inadvertent waiver of privileges
