## Production Format Considerations

**Native Format:** Best for ESI - preserves metadata
**PDF:** Standard for paper documents
**TIFF:** Common for large productions with Bates numbering

---

## Key Rules

**Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.350:**
- Response: 30 days from service
- Objections: Must state specific grounds
- Production: As kept in usual course of business OR organized by category
- Privilege log required for withheld documents

**Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.280(b):** Proportionality applies to all discovery
