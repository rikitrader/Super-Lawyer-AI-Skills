          [PLAINTIFF'S/DEFENDANT'S] FIRST SET OF INTERROGATORIES
                        TO [DEFENDANT/PLAINTIFF]

        Pursuant to Florida Rule of Civil Procedure 1.340, [Plaintiff/Defendant],
{{PROPOUNDING_PARTY}}, hereby propounds the following interrogatories to
[Defendant/Plaintiff], {{RESPONDING_PARTY}}, to be answered separately and fully,
in writing, under oath, within thirty (30) days of service hereof.

                            DEFINITIONS

        As used in these interrogatories:

        1.      "YOU," "YOUR," and "{{RESPONDING_PARTY}}" refer to the party
to whom these interrogatories are directed, including all agents, employees,
representatives, attorneys (to the extent not privileged), and any other persons
acting on behalf of or subject to the control of that party.

        2.      "DOCUMENT" means any written, printed, typed, recorded, or
graphic matter, however produced or reproduced, including but not limited to:
correspondence, memoranda, notes, reports, studies, analyses, contracts,
agreements, invoices, receipts, checks, records, diaries, calendars, and
electronically stored information.

        3.      "COMMUNICATION" means any oral, written, or electronic exchange
of information, including but not limited to: conversations, meetings, telephone
calls, emails, text messages, and social media messages.

        4.      "IDENTIFY" when used with respect to:
                a.      A natural person: State the person's full name, last
                        known address, telephone number, and relationship to any
                        party;
                b.      A business entity: State the full name, address,
                        telephone number, and the name of its representative
                        most knowledgeable about the subject matter;
                c.      A document: State the date, author, recipient(s), type
                        of document, subject matter, and present location.

        5.      "RELATE TO" or "RELATING TO" means concerning, referring to,
discussing, describing, reflecting, evidencing, constituting, or in any way
pertaining to.

        6.      The "SUBJECT MATTER" refers to the transactions, events, and
claims alleged in the pleadings in this action.

        7.      The term "AND" shall be construed as "or" and vice versa, and
the singular shall include the plural and vice versa, as necessary to make the
interrogatory inclusive rather than exclusive.

                            INSTRUCTIONS

        1.      Each interrogatory shall be answered separately and fully under
oath, in accordance with Florida Rule of Civil Procedure 1.340(a).

        2.      If you object to any interrogatory, state with specificity the
grounds for the objection and whether any responsive information is being
withheld on the basis of the objection. Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.340(b).

        3.      If you claim any information is protected by privilege, identify
the information, the privilege claimed, and provide sufficient information to
enable assessment of the privilege claim.

        4.      These interrogatories are continuing in nature. You are required
to supplement your responses if you obtain additional information. Fla. R. Civ.
P. 1.280(e).

        5.      Unless otherwise specified, these interrogatories cover the time
period from {{START_DATE}} to the present.

                          INTERROGATORIES

                     IDENTIFICATION AND BACKGROUND

        INTERROGATORY NO. 1:
        State your full legal name, all other names by which you have been known,
your date of birth, current address, and all addresses at which you have resided
during the past five (5) years.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 2:
        If you are a corporation, limited liability company, partnership, or
other business entity, state:
                a.      Your full legal name and all trade names or d/b/a names;
                b.      Your state of incorporation or organization;
                c.      Your principal place of business;
                d.      The identity of all officers, directors, members,
                        managers, or partners during the relevant time period;
                e.      Your registered agent and address for service.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 3:
        Identify all persons who have knowledge of facts relevant to this action,
and for each person, describe the subject matter of their knowledge.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 4:
        Identify all persons who participated in, witnessed, or have knowledge of
the [transaction/incident/events] at issue in this action.

                     FACTUAL BACKGROUND OF CLAIMS

        INTERROGATORY NO. 5:
        Describe in detail all facts that support [Plaintiff's claims /
Defendant's defenses] in this action.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 6:
        Describe the complete factual basis for each [allegation in the Complaint
/ denial or affirmative defense in the Answer].

        INTERROGATORY NO. 7:
        Identify all documents that relate to the [claims / defenses] in this
action.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 8:
        Describe all communications between the parties relating to the subject
matter of this action, including the date, participants, method, and substance
of each communication.

                           CONTRACTS
              [FOR CONTRACT CLAIMS - DELETE IF NOT APPLICABLE]

        INTERROGATORY NO. 9:
        Describe the [Contract/Agreement] at issue, including:
                a.      The date(s) of formation;
                b.      The parties;
                c.      The material terms;
                d.      Whether it was written or oral;
                e.      All amendments or modifications.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 10:
        Describe all actions you took to perform your obligations under the
[Contract/Agreement].

        INTERROGATORY NO. 11:
        Describe all alleged breaches of the [Contract/Agreement], including:
                a.      Each specific breach;
                b.      The date of each breach;
                c.      How each breach was discovered;
                d.      All communications regarding each breach.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 12:
        Identify all persons who participated in the negotiation, drafting, or
execution of the [Contract/Agreement].

                           DAMAGES

        INTERROGATORY NO. 13:
        Describe in detail all damages you claim in this action, including:
                a.      Each category of damage;
                b.      The amount claimed for each category;
                c.      The method of calculation;
                d.      All documents supporting each category.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 14:
        Identify all economic losses you claim, including lost profits, and
describe how such losses were calculated.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 15:
        Identify all payments, credits, or offsets that have been received or
should be applied to reduce the damages claimed.

                         LEGAL CONTENTIONS

        INTERROGATORY NO. 16:
        State the legal basis for each claim asserted in your [Complaint /
Counterclaim], including all statutes, rules, regulations, and case law upon
which you rely.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 17:
        State the factual and legal basis for each affirmative defense asserted
in your Answer.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 18:
        Identify all statutes of limitations you contend are applicable to the
claims in this action, and state when you contend each cause of action accrued.

                          WITNESSES

        INTERROGATORY NO. 19:
        Identify all witnesses you may call at trial, and for each witness state:
                a.      Full name and address;
                b.      Relationship to any party;
                c.      Subject matter of expected testimony.

        INTERROGATORY NO. 20:
        Identify all experts you have retained or consulted in connection with
this action, and for each expert state:
                a.      Full name and address;
                b.      Area of expertise;
                c.      Subject matter of opinions;
                d.      Compensation arrangements.

                           INSURANCE

        INTERROGATORY NO. 21:
        Identify all insurance policies that may provide coverage for the claims
in this action, and for each policy state:
                a.      Name of insurer;
                b.      Policy number;
                c.      Policy period;
                d.      Coverage limits;
                e.      Any coverage disputes or reservations of rights.

                    PRIOR LITIGATION AND CLAIMS

        INTERROGATORY NO. 22:
        Describe all other lawsuits, arbitrations, or administrative proceedings

        INTERROGATORY NO. 23:
        Describe any claims, disputes, or lawsuits between the parties to this

        INTERROGATORY NO. 25:

## Key Rules

**Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.340:**
- Limit: 30 interrogatories including subparts
- Response: 30 days from service
- Objections: Must state specific grounds with detailed reasoning
- Supplementation: Continuing duty under Rule 1.280(e)
