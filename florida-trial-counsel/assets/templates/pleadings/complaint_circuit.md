# Complaint Template - Florida Circuit Court

---

## Document Information

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Purpose** | Template for initiating civil action in Florida Circuit Court |
| **When to Use** | Civil disputes exceeding $50,000; equity matters; real property disputes |
| **Filing Notes** | File with Clerk of Circuit Court; pay filing fee; arrange service |
| **Rule Reference** | Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.100, 1.110, 1.130 |

---

## Completion Checklist

- [ ] Verify court caption format (ordinal judicial circuit)
- [ ] Confirm jurisdictional amount ($50,000+)
- [ ] Verify venue under Fla. Stat. Ch. 47
- [ ] Attach all written instruments per Rule 1.130
- [ ] Plead ultimate facts (not conclusions)
- [ ] If fraud claim: plead with particularity
- [ ] State performance of conditions precedent
- [ ] Include specific damages
- [ ] Cite statutory/contractual basis for attorney's fees
- [ ] Include jury demand if desired
- [ ] Sign and include Florida Bar number
- [ ] Prepare summons for service

## Risk Flags

- [ ] Confirm standing to sue
- [ ] Verify proper capacity (individual, entity, representative)
