# Complaint - Fraud / Fraudulent Misrepresentation

---
purpose: "Initiate fraud action in Florida state court"
when_to_use: "When defendant made false statements inducing detrimental reliance"
court_level: "circuit or county (based on amount)"
rule_reference: "Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.110, 1.120(b) - MUST PLEAD WITH PARTICULARITY"
elements:
  - "False statement of material fact"
  - "Defendant knew statement was false (or reckless disregard)"
  - "Intent to induce reliance"
  - "Justifiable reliance by plaintiff"
  - "Damages resulting from reliance"
key_case: "Lopez-Infante v. Union Cent. Life Ins. Co., 809 So. 2d 13 (Fla. 3d DCA 2002)"
sol: "4 years from discovery (12-year max) - Fla. Stat. §§ 95.11(3)(j), 95.031(2)(a)"
---

## CRITICAL: PARTICULARITY REQUIREMENT

**Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.120(b):** "In all averments of fraud or mistake, the circumstances constituting fraud or mistake shall be stated with such particularity as the circumstances may permit."

**Must allege:**
- WHO made the misrepresentation
- WHAT was said (specific statement)
- WHEN it was said
- WHERE it was said
- WHY it was false
- HOW plaintiff relied

## Key Case Law

- *Lopez-Infante v. Union Cent. Life Ins. Co.*, 809 So. 2d 13 (Fla. 3d DCA 2002)
- *Simon v. Celebration Co.*, 883 So. 2d 826 (Fla. 5th DCA 2004)

**Particularity Requirement:**
- *Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.120(b)*

**Justifiable Reliance:**
- *Besett v. Basnett*, 389 So. 2d 995 (Fla. 1980)

**Negligent Misrepresentation:**
- *Restatement (Second) of Torts § 552* (adopted in Florida)

**Fraud in the Inducement:**
- *Mettler, Inc. v. Ellen Tracy, Inc.*, 648 So. 2d 253 (Fla. 2d DCA 1994)

## Statute of Limitations

**Fla. Stat. § 95.11(3)(j):** 4 years
**Fla. Stat. § 95.031(2)(a):** Discovery rule applies
**Maximum:** 12 years from date facts giving rise to cause of action occurred
