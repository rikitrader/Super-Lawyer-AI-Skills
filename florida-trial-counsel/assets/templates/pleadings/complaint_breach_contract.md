# Complaint - Breach of Contract

---
purpose: "Initiate breach of contract action in Florida state court"
when_to_use: "When defendant has breached a written or oral contract"
court_level: "circuit or county (based on amount)"
rule_reference: "Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.110, 1.130"
elements:
  - "Valid contract existed"
  - "Plaintiff performed or was excused"
  - "Defendant breached"
  - "Plaintiff suffered damages"
key_case: "Friedman v. New York Life Ins. Co., 985 So. 2d 56 (Fla. 4th DCA 2008)"
---

## Required Attachments

For written contract claims, **must attach** (Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.130):
- [ ] The contract
- [ ] All amendments and addenda
- [ ] Any guaranties
- [ ] Relevant correspondence regarding breach

If cannot attach, explain in complaint why document cannot be attached.

## Key Case Law

**Elements:**
- *Friedman v. New York Life Ins. Co.*, 985 So. 2d 56, 58 (Fla. 4th DCA 2008)
- *Beck v. Lazard Freres & Co.*, 175 F.3d 913, 914 (11th Cir. 1999)

**Contract Formation:**
- *St. Joe Corp. v. McIver*, 875 So. 2d 375 (Fla. 2004)

**Oral Contracts:**
- *Liza Danielle, Inc. v. Jamko, Inc.*, 408 So. 2d 735 (Fla. 3d DCA 1982)

**Conditions Precedent:**
- *Fla. R. Civ. P. 1.120(c)* (may aver generally)

**Lost Profits:**
- *W.W. Gay Mech. Contractor, Inc. v. Wharfside Two, Ltd.*, 545 So. 2d 1348 (Fla. 1989)
