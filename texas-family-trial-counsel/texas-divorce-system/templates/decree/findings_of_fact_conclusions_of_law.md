## PROCEDURE FOR REQUESTING FINDINGS

**TRCP 296-299 Requirements:**

| Action | Deadline | Rule |
|--------|----------|------|
| Request for Findings | Within 20 days of judgment | TRCP 296 |
| Court Files Findings | Within 20 days of request | TRCP 297 |
| Request for Additional Findings | Within 10 days of original findings | TRCP 298 |
| Court Files Additional | Within 10 days of request | TRCP 298 |

**If Findings Requested But Not Filed:** Deemed findings in support of judgment

## STRATEGIC CONSIDERATIONS

### Findings Favorable to Client - Request Specificity On:

| Issue | Why Important |
|-------|---------------|
| Best interest factors | Supports custody ruling |
| Income findings | Supports support amount |
| Property values | Supports division |
| Credibility | Supports favorable testimony |
| Fault findings | Supports unequal division |

### Findings Unfavorable - Consider Challenging:

| Issue | Challenge Strategy |
|-------|-------------------|
| Incorrect facts | Request correction via motion |
| Missing findings | Request additional findings |
| Insufficient evidence | Preserve for appeal |
| Legal error | Preserve for appeal |
