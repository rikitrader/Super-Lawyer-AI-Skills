## Affidavit Preparation Notes

**Key Requirements:**
- Must be based on personal knowledge
- Must be sworn/notarized
- Facts must be true and accurate
- Avoid legal conclusions—state facts

**Evidence Considerations:**
- This affidavit is evidence at the hearing
- Opposing party may cross-examine on contents
- Inconsistencies can be used for impeachment
- Keep statements factual and provable

---

## Completion Checklist

- [ ] All facts verified with client
- [ ] Income figures documented
- [ ] Expenses documented
- [ ] Child-related facts accurate
- [ ] Notarization arranged
- [ ] Client reviewed and approved
- [ ] Filed with motion

## Risk Flags

- [ ] Facts not verifiable
- [ ] Income figures unsupported
- [ ] Overstatements or exaggerations
- [ ] Legal conclusions instead of facts
- [ ] Missing notarization
