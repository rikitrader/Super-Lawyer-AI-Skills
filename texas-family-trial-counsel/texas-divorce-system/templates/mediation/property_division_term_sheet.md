## DIVISION FRAMEWORK

**Texas Law:** Just and right division (TFC § 7.001) - NOT necessarily equal

**Factors Court May Consider:**
- Disparity in earning capacity
- Fault in breakup of marriage
- Health and age of parties
- Education and employment skills
- Attorney's fees paid from community
- Separate property of each
- Nature of property being divided
