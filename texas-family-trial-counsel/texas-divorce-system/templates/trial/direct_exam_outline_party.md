## DIRECT EXAMINATION PRINCIPLES

**Remember:**
- Open-ended questions (Who, What, When, Where, Why, How)
- Let witness tell the story
- Don't lead on material matters
- Listen to answers
- Follow up when needed
- Maintain pace - don't rush
- Make eye contact with witness and judge

---
