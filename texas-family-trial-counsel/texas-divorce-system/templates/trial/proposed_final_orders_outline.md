### B. Rights and Duties - Parent 1

```
IT IS ORDERED that [PARENT 1], as a parent joint managing conservator,
shall have the following EXCLUSIVE rights:

1. The right to designate the primary residence of the child within
   [COUNTY] County [and contiguous counties] [or other restriction];
2. The right to consent to invasive medical, dental, and surgical
   treatment of the child;
3. The right to consent to psychiatric and psychological treatment
   of the child;
4. The right to receive and give receipt for child support;
5. [Other exclusive rights]

IT IS ORDERED that [PARENT 1] shall have the following EXCLUSIVE duties:

1. The duty to [specify if any];
```

### C. Rights and Duties - Parent 2

```
IT IS ORDERED that [PARENT 2], as a parent joint managing conservator,
shall have the following EXCLUSIVE rights:

1. [Specify if any]

IT IS ORDERED that [PARENT 2] shall have the following EXCLUSIVE duties:

1. [Specify if any]
```

### D. Joint Rights and Duties

```
IT IS ORDERED that [PARENT 1] and [PARENT 2] shall have the following
JOINT rights:

1. The right to access medical, dental, psychological, and educational
   records of the child;
2. The right to consult with physicians, dentists, psychologists,
   teachers, and other persons treating or providing services to
   the child;
3. The right to attend school activities and extracurricular activities;
4. The right to consent to emergency medical treatment;
5. [Other joint rights]

IT IS ORDERED that [PARENT 1] and [PARENT 2] shall have the following
JOINT duties:

1. The duty to inform the other conservator of significant information
   concerning the health, education, and welfare of the child;
2. The duty to confer before making decisions concerning the health,
   education, and welfare of the child;
```

The real property located at [ADDRESS], legally described as [LEGAL

[TIMEFRAME] to remove [OTHER PARTY] from all liability. If refinancing
is not completed within the stated time, [consequence].
