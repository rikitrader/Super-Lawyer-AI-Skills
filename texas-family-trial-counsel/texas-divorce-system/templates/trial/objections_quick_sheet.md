## OBJECTION PROTOCOL

**Making Objections:**
1. Stand (unless court allows seated)
2. State: "Objection, Your Honor"
3. State ground: "[specific objection]"
4. Wait for ruling
5. If overruled, say "May I have a running objection?" (for repeated issue)

**Responding to Objections:**
1. Wait for court to ask (or proceed if allowed)
2. Briefly state basis for admissibility
3. Cite rule if helpful

## COMMON OBJECTIONS

### RELEVANCE (TRE 401-403)

| Objection | When to Use | Response |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **"Relevance"** | Evidence has no bearing on issues | "Goes to [issue]" / "Relevant to [claim/defense]" |
| **"TRE 403 - Prejudice"** | Probative value outweighed by unfair prejudice | "Probative value substantial" / "Not unfairly prejudicial" |
| **"Cumulative"** | Same evidence already admitted | "This adds [specific new info]" |
| **"Confusing"** | Will mislead trier of fact | "Clear and straightforward" |

---

### HEARSAY (TRE 801-806)

| Objection | When to Use | Response |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **"Hearsay"** | Out-of-court statement offered for truth | See exceptions below |

**Hearsay Exceptions (TRE 803 - Availability Immaterial):**

| Exception | Rule | Use |
|-----------|------|-----|
| Present sense impression | 803(1) | Statement describing event while perceiving it |
| Excited utterance | 803(2) | Statement relating to startling event |
| State of mind | 803(3) | Statement of declarant's mental/emotional condition |
| Medical diagnosis | 803(4) | Statements for medical treatment |
| Recorded recollection | 803(5) | Witness can't recall, but recorded when fresh |
| **Business record** | 803(6) | Record kept in regular course of business |
| Absence of record | 803(7) | Proof matter not recorded |
| **Public record** | 803(8) | Government agency records |
| Vital statistics | 803(9) | Birth, death, marriage records |
| Absence of public record | 803(10) | Proof record doesn't exist |
| Religious records | 803(11) | Church records |
| Family records | 803(13) | Family history documents |
| Property records | 803(14-15) | Property documents |
| Ancient documents | 803(16) | 20+ years old, authentic |
| Market reports | 803(17) | Published compilations |
| Learned treatises | 803(18) | Established authority (with expert) |
| Reputation | 803(19-21) | Family, boundaries, character |
| Judgment of conviction | 803(22) | Felony conviction |
| Judgment - family matters | 803(23) | Personal/family history |

**Not Hearsay (TRE 801(e)):**

| Category | Rule | Use |
|----------|------|-----|
| Prior inconsistent | 801(e)(1)(A) | Impeachment, given under oath |
| Prior consistent | 801(e)(1)(B) | Rebut fabrication charge |
| Identification | 801(e)(1)(C) | Prior ID of person |
| **Opposing party statement** | 801(e)(2) | Statement by party-opponent |

### WITNESS TESTIMONY (TRE 601-615)

| Objection | When to Use | Response |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **"Leading"** | Suggests answer on direct | "Preliminary matter" / "Hostile witness" |
| **"Speculation"** | Witness guessing | "Based on personal knowledge" |
| **"Lack of personal knowledge"** | Witness doesn't know | "Witness has foundation" |
| **"Narrative"** | Rambling, non-responsive | "Witness is answering the question" |
| **"Non-responsive"** | Answer doesn't address question | "Move to strike" |
| **"Asked and answered"** | Same question repeated | "Different aspect" / "Clarification" |
| **"Compound"** | Multiple questions | Rephrase |
| **"Argumentative"** | Arguing with witness | Rephrase |
| **"Assumes facts not in evidence"** | Question assumes unproven fact | "Foundation laid through [testimony]" |
| **"Calls for legal conclusion"** | Asking witness for legal opinion | Rephrase as factual |
| **"Beyond the scope"** | Cross exceeds direct | "Within scope" / "Impeachment" |
| **"Improper impeachment"** | Improper impeachment method | "Proper under TRE 613" |

---

### AUTHENTICATION (TRE 901-903)

| Objection | When to Use | Response |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **"Lack of foundation"** | No proper authentication | "Foundation laid through [method]" |
| **"Not authenticated"** | Document not properly identified | "[Method] establishes authenticity" |
| **"Best evidence"** | Original not produced (TRE 1002) | "Original unavailable" / "Duplicate admissible" |

**Authentication Methods (TRE 901(b)):**
1. Testimony of witness with knowledge
2. Non-expert handwriting opinion
3. Expert comparison
4. Distinctive characteristics (emails, texts)
5. Voice identification
6. Telephone conversation
7. Public records
8. Ancient documents
9. Process or system
10. Methods provided by statute

**Self-Authenticating (TRE 902):**
- Certified copies
- Official publications
- Newspapers/periodicals
- Trade inscriptions
- Acknowledged documents
- Commercial paper
- Certified business records

---

### PRIVILEGE (TRE 501-513)

| Objection | When to Use | Response |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **"Attorney-client privilege"** | Communication with lawyer | Establish waiver/exception |
| **"Work product"** | Litigation preparation materials | Show substantial need |
| **"Spousal privilege"** | Communications between spouses | Often waived in divorce |
| **"Physician-patient"** | Medical communications | Exception in family cases |

---

### CHARACTER EVIDENCE (TRE 404-405)

| Objection | When to Use | Response |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **"Improper character evidence"** | Character to prove conforming conduct | "Not offered for character" |
| **"404(b) violation"** | Prior bad acts | "Offered for [motive/plan/knowledge/etc.]" |

**TRE 404(b) Permitted Purposes:**
- Motive
- Opportunity
- Intent
- Preparation
- Plan
- Knowledge
- Identity
- Absence of mistake

---

### EXPERT TESTIMONY (TRE 702-705)

| Objection | When to Use | Response |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **"Not qualified"** | Expert lacks qualifications | "Qualified by [education/experience]" |
| **"Unreliable methodology"** | Method not accepted | "Generally accepted / peer reviewed" |
| **"Insufficient facts"** | Opinion lacks factual basis | "Based on [facts/data]" |
| **"Net opinion"** | Conclusion without basis | "Basis explained" |

## TRIAL QUICK REFERENCE

### Offering Exhibits

```
1. "I'm showing the witness what's been marked as [Party's] Exhibit [#]."
2. "Do you recognize this?"
3. [Establish foundation]
4. "Your Honor, I offer [Party's] Exhibit [#] into evidence."
5. [Court asks for objection]
6. [Ruling]
7. [If admitted] "Permission to publish to the Court?"
```

### Impeachment with Prior Statement

```
1. "You gave a deposition on [date]?"
2. "You were under oath?"
3. "You were asked questions and gave answers?"
4. "Let me direct your attention to page [X], line [Y]."
5. [Provide copy to witness, opposing counsel, court]
6. "Were you asked this question and did you give this answer?"
7. [Read Q&A]
8. "That was your testimony?"
9. [Stop - point made]
```

### Motion to Strike

```
"Your Honor, I move to strike [the testimony/that answer] as
[hearsay/non-responsive/speculation] and ask the Court to
instruct [the jury/itself] to disregard."
```
