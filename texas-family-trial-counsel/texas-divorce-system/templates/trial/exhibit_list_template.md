## EXHIBIT PREPARATION CHECKLIST

### For Each Exhibit:
- [ ] Original obtained
- [ ] Copies made (4 sets: Judge, witness, opposing, file)
- [ ] Exhibit sticker/number applied
- [ ] Authentication method identified
- [ ] Witness identified to introduce
- [ ] Foundation questions prepared
- [ ] Anticipated objections noted

### Physical Organization:
- [ ] Exhibits in numerical order
- [ ] Tabbed for quick access
- [ ] Index at front of binder
- [ ] Extra copies available

## AUTHENTICATION REFERENCE

| Method | Rule | Use For |
|--------|------|---------|
| Testimony | TRE 901(b)(1) | Documents witness can identify |
| Self-authenticating | TRE 902 | Certified copies, official records |
| Business Record | TRE 803(6) | Records kept in regular course |
| Public Record | TRE 803(8) | Government records |
| Distinctive characteristics | TRE 901(b)(4) | Emails, texts, social media |
| Expert | TRE 702 | Expert reports, opinions |
