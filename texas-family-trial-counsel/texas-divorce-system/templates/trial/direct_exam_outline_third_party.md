## LAY WITNESS EXAMINATION

### I. INTRODUCTION

**Goal:** Establish who witness is and why they can testify

```
Q: Please state your full name.
A:

Q: Where do you live?
A:

Q: What do you do for a living?
A:

Q: How do you know [Client]?
A:

Q: How long have you known [Client]?
A:

Q: How do you know [Opposing Party]?
A:

Q: How often do you see [Client] and their family?
A:
```

---

### II. FAMILY MEMBER WITNESS

**Goal:** Observations of parenting, children, relationship

#### A. Observations of Client as Parent

```
Q: Have you had the opportunity to observe [Client] with [his/her] children?
A:

Q: On approximately how many occasions?
A:

Q: Where have you observed them together?
A:

Q: Can you describe what you've observed about how [Client] interacts with the children?
A:

Q: Have you observed [Client] disciplining the children?
A:

Q: How would you describe [Client]'s discipline style?
A:

Q: Have you observed [Client] helping the children with homework?
A:

Q: Have you seen [Client] take the children to activities?
A:

Q: How would you describe [Client] as a parent?
A:
```

#### B. Observations of Children

```
Q: How would you describe the children's relationship with [Client]?
A:

Q: How do the children act when they are with [Client]?
A:

Q: Have you noticed any changes in the children since the separation?
A:

Q: [If applicable] Have the children said anything to you about their living situation?
A:
```

#### C. Observations of Opposing Party (if applicable)

```
Q: Have you observed [Opposing Party] with the children?
A:

Q: What have you observed?
A:

Q: Did you witness any specific incident involving [Opposing Party]?
A:

Q: Please describe what you saw/heard.
A:

Q: When did this occur?
A:

Q: Where did this occur?
A:

Q: Who else was present?
A:
```

### III. FRIEND/NEIGHBOR WITNESS

**Goal:** Corroborate client's version of events, character

#### A. Relationship Establishment

```
Q: How do you know [Client]?
A:

Q: How long have you been [friends/neighbors]?
A:

Q: How often do you interact with [Client]?
A:
```

#### B. Specific Observations

```
Q: Were you present on [date] when [event] occurred?
A:

Q: Where were you?
A:

Q: What did you observe?
A:

Q: What did you see?
A:

Q: What did you hear?
A:

Q: Who was present?
A:

Q: What happened next?
A:
```

#### C. Character Evidence (if admissible)

```
Q: How well do you know [Client]?
A:

Q: Based on your interactions, what is [Client]'s reputation for [truthfulness/being a good parent/etc.]?
A:
```

### V. TEACHER/SCHOOL WITNESS

**Goal:** Children's welfare, parental involvement

```

Q: Have you met [Opposing Party]?

### VI. THERAPIST/COUNSELOR WITNESS

**Goal:** Mental health, treatment, recommendations (within privilege limits)

*Note: Privilege issues - ensure proper waiver before examination*

```
Q: What are your professional qualifications?
A:

Q: What is your educational background?
A:

Q: Are you licensed in Texas?
A:

Q: How long have you been practicing?
A:

A:

Q: For how long?
A:

A:

Q: What were the presenting issues?
A:

Q: What is your assessment of [Client's/Child's] current condition?
A:

A:

## EXPERT WITNESS EXAMINATION

### VII. EXPERT QUALIFICATION

```
Q: Please state your name.
A:

Q: What is your occupation?
A:

Q: What is your educational background?
A:

Q: What degrees do you hold?
A:

Q: Are you licensed in your field?
A:

Q: In what state?
A:

Q: How long have you been licensed?
A:

Q: What is your area of specialty?
A:

Q: Have you received any specialized training?
A:

Q: Have you published any articles or books in your field?
A:

Q: Have you previously testified as an expert?
A:

Q: Approximately how many times?
A:

Your Honor, I tender [Witness] as an expert in [field].
```

### VIII. EXPERT METHODOLOGY

```
Q: Were you retained in this case?
A:

Q: By whom?
A:

Q: What were you asked to do?
A:

Q: What methodology did you use?
A:

Q: Is that methodology generally accepted in your field?
A:

Q: What materials did you review?
A:

Q: Did you interview anyone?
A:

Q: What tests did you administer? (if applicable)
A:
```

### IX. EXPERT OPINIONS

```
Q: Did you form an opinion based on your work in this case?
A:

Q: What is your opinion regarding [issue]?
A:

Q: What is the basis for that opinion?
A:

Q: Do you hold this opinion to a reasonable degree of [professional certainty]?
A:

[Continue for each opinion]
```

## RECORDS CUSTODIAN EXAMINATION

### X. BUSINESS RECORDS FOUNDATION

```
Q: What is your name?
A:

Q: Where do you work?
A:

Q: What is your position?
A:

Q: Are you the records custodian for [Company]?
A:

Q: What are your duties as records custodian?
A:

[EXHIBIT: Records]

Q: I'm showing you what's been marked as [Party]'s Exhibit [#]. Do you recognize these documents?
A:

Q: What are they?
A:

Q: Are these records kept in the regular course of business?
A:

Q: Is it the regular practice of [Company] to make and keep such records?
A:

Q: Were these records made at or near the time of the events recorded?
A:

Q: Were they made by someone with knowledge of the events?
A:

Q: Are these records accurate?
A:

Your Honor, I offer [Party]'s Exhibit [#] as a business record under TRE 803(6).
```
