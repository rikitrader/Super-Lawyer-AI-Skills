## MODIFICATION STANDARDS

**General Standard (TFC § 156.101):**
Modification requires showing:
1. Circumstances have materially and substantially changed since the prior order; AND
2. Modification is in the child's best interest

**One-Year Waiting Period Exception (TFC § 156.102):**
Within one year of prior order, must show:
- Child's present environment endangers physical health or emotional development; AND
- Modification would be positive improvement

**Change of Primary Residence (TFC § 156.101(a)(1)):**
If requesting change of person with right to designate residence, must show:
- Person with right to designate has voluntarily relinquished primary care; OR
- Material and substantial change + best interest
