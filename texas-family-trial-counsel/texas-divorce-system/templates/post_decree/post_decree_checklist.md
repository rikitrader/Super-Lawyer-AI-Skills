## FUTURE MODIFICATION TRIGGERS

### Circumstances That May Warrant Modification:

**Child Support:**
- [ ] 3 years since order + 20%/$100 difference
- [ ] Material change in income
- [ ] Change in children's needs
- [ ] Change in custody/possession

**Custody/Possession:**
- [ ] Material and substantial change in circumstances
- [ ] Child's needs changed
- [ ] Parent's circumstances changed
- [ ] Relocation

### Note for Client:
If any of these circumstances occur, contact attorney to discuss modification options.

## ENFORCEMENT NOTES

### If Other Party Fails to Comply:

| Violation | First Step | Escalation |
|-----------|------------|------------|
| Missed support payment | Document; wait for pattern | Motion to Enforce |
| Denied possession | Document; demand in writing | Motion to Enforce |
| Failed property transfer | Demand letter | Motion to Enforce |
| Failed to refinance | Demand letter | Motion to Enforce + remedies |

### Documentation to Maintain:

- [ ] Copy of decree
- [ ] All payment records
- [ ] Communication log
- [ ] Possession exchange log
- [ ] Violation documentation
- [ ] Expense receipts (unreimbursed medical)
