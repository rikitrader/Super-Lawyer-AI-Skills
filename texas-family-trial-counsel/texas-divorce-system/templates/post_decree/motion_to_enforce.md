## ENFORCEMENT OPTIONS

| Remedy | Use For | Authority |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| **Contempt** | Any order violation | TFC Ch. 157 |
| **Income Withholding** | Child support | TFC Ch. 158 |
| **License Suspension** | Support arrears | TFC Ch. 232 |
| **Lien** | Support arrears | TFC Ch. 157 |
| **Money Judgment** | Support/property | TFC § 157.263 |
| **Clarification** | Ambiguous orders | TFC § 157.421 |

**Contempt Penalties:**
- Up to 6 months jail per violation
- Up to $500 fine per violation
- Attorney's fees
- Community service

## SERVICE REQUIREMENTS

**For Contempt:** Personal service required (TRCP 21a does not apply)
- Citation must be personally served
- Respondent must be personally served with motion
- Alternative service requires court order

---

## Completion Checklist

- [ ] Prior order attached
- [ ] Each violation specifically alleged
- [ ] Dates and amounts precise
- [ ] Ability to comply alleged
- [ ] Arrearage calculation attached
- [ ] Verification signed
- [ ] Personal service arranged

## Risk Flags

- [ ] Order terms ambiguous (may need clarification first)
- [ ] Respondent has defense (inability to pay)
- [ ] Violations not clearly documented
- [ ] Service problems anticipated
- [ ] Respondent judgment-proof
