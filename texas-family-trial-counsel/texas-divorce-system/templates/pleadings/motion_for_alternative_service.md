     1.02 Citation was issued for service on Respondent,

     1.03 Despite diligent efforts, Petitioner has been unable to

                    II. DUE DILIGENCE EFFORTS

     2.01 Petitioner has exercised due diligence in attempting to
locate Respondent and effect service. The following efforts have
been made:

          a. Attempted personal service at Respondent's last known
          address: {{LAST_KNOWN_ADDRESS}};

          b. Attempted personal service at Respondent's last known
          workplace: {{LAST_KNOWN_EMPLOYER}};

          c. Searched public records for current address, including:
               - Texas driver's license records
               - Voter registration records
               - Property records
               - Court records

          d. Contacted known family members of Respondent:
               - {{FAMILY_MEMBER_1}}
               - {{FAMILY_MEMBER_2}}

          e. Searched social media accounts;

          f. Conducted internet search for current contact information;

          g. Checked post office for forwarding address;

          h. [Additional efforts as applicable]

     2.02 A detailed Affidavit of Due Diligence is attached hereto
as Exhibit A and incorporated by reference.

     2.03 Despite these efforts, Respondent's current whereabouts
remain unknown.

[ALTERNATIVE - IF RESPONDENT IS EVADING SERVICE:]
[
     2.03 Service has been attempted on [number] occasions at
[address(es)], but Respondent has avoided and evaded service.
The process server has reported the following:

          a. [Details of evasion attempts]
]

                    III. REQUEST FOR ALTERNATIVE SERVICE

     3.01 Petitioner requests the Court authorize service on
Respondent by one of the following methods:

[OPTION A - SERVICE BY PUBLICATION:]

          Service by Publication: Petitioner requests that the Court
          authorize service upon Respondent by publication in a
          newspaper of general circulation in {{COUNTY}} County, Texas,
          [or in the county of Respondent's last known address], once
          each week for four consecutive weeks as provided by Rule 117
          of the Texas Rules of Civil Procedure.

[OPTION B - SERVICE BY POSTING:]

          Service by Posting: Petitioner requests that the Court
          authorize service upon Respondent by posting citation at
          the courthouse door of {{COUNTY}} County, Texas, for a period
          of time as required by law.

[OPTION C - SUBSTITUTED SERVICE AT ALTERNATIVE ADDRESS:]

          Substituted Service: Petitioner requests that the Court
          authorize substituted service upon Respondent by leaving
          a true copy of the citation with petition at [specific
          address] with [specific person] who is a person over
          sixteen years of age at [relationship to Respondent].

                          IV. AUTHORITY

     4.01 Rule 109 of the Texas Rules of Civil Procedure provides
that when a party's residence is unknown, service may be had by
publication.

     4.02 Rule 117 of the Texas Rules of Civil Procedure governs
citation by publication.

     4.03 Rule 106 of the Texas Rules of Civil Procedure authorizes
substituted service when the Court determines that service by
personal service or certified mail will not be effective.

                          V. PRAYER

     WHEREFORE, PREMISES CONSIDERED, Petitioner respectfully requests
that the Court:

     1. Grant this Motion for Substituted Service / Alternative Service;

     2. Authorize service upon Respondent by [publication/posting/
     substituted service];

     3. Issue an Order Authorizing Substituted Service;

     4. Grant such other and further relief to which Petitioner may
     be justly entitled.

## Completion Checklist

**Due Diligence Documentation:**
- [ ] Last known address searched
- [ ] Last known employer contacted
- [ ] DPS records checked
- [ ] Voter registration checked
- [ ] Property records checked
- [ ] Family members contacted
- [ ] Social media searched
- [ ] Post office checked
- [ ] All efforts documented with dates

**Filing:**
- [ ] Motion prepared
- [ ] Affidavit prepared and notarized
- [ ] Proposed order prepared
- [ ] E-filed with court
- [ ] Hearing requested if required

**After Order Granted:**
- [ ] Obtain signed order
- [ ] Arrange publication/posting
- [ ] Monitor publication dates
- [ ] File proof of publication/posting
- [ ] Calculate answer deadline

## Risk Flags

- [ ] Insufficient due diligence documented
- [ ] Address actually known (improper use)
- [ ] Publication in wrong county
- [ ] Proof of publication not timely filed
- [ ] Answer deadline miscalculated

---

*For attorney review before filing. Ensure all due diligence efforts are thoroughly documented.*
