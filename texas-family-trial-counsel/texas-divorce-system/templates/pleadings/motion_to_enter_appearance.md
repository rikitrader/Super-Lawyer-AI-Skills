## Completion Checklist

**Before Filing:**
- [ ] Client has signed engagement letter
- [ ] Conflict check completed
- [ ] Retainer received
- [ ] Prior counsel notified (if substitution)
- [ ] Case file obtained (if substitution)

**Filing:**
- [ ] E-file with district clerk
- [ ] Serve on all parties/counsel
- [ ] Certificate of service completed

**After Filing:**
- [ ] Update contact information with court
- [ ] Register for e-service notifications
- [ ] Review case file and pending deadlines
- [ ] Calendar all upcoming dates

## Risk Flags

- [ ] Prior counsel withdrawal not complete
- [ ] Pending deadline missed in transition
- [ ] Client obligations to prior counsel unclear
- [ ] Former counsel signature needed but unavailable
