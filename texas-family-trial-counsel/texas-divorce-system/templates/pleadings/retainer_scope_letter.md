## 5. Termination

### By Client

You may terminate this engagement at any time by written notice. You will remain responsible for fees and costs incurred through termination.

### By Attorney

We may withdraw from representation if:
- You fail to pay fees or costs as agreed
- You fail to cooperate or follow advice
- Continued representation would violate professional rules
- Other good cause exists

We will provide reasonable notice and seek court permission if required.

### Upon Termination

Upon termination:
- We will return your file and documents
- You will pay outstanding fees and costs
- We will cooperate in transition to new counsel
