## Important Notes for Respondent

**READ CAREFULLY BEFORE SIGNING:**

1. **Voluntary Act** - Signing this waiver is completely voluntary. You have the right to require formal service of citation.

2. **Receipt of Petition** - By signing, you acknowledge that you have received a copy of the divorce petition filed against you.

3. **Answer Deadline** - Unless you also waive the answer deadline (optional paragraph), you still have until the first Monday after 20 days from the petition's filing date to file an answer.

4. **Right to Attorney** - You have the right to consult with an attorney before signing this document.

5. **Consequences** - If you fail to file an answer and a default judgment is taken, you may lose rights regarding property division, child custody, child support, and other matters.

6. **Timing** - This waiver must be signed AFTER the petition has been filed with the court.

## Completion Checklist

**Before Presenting to Respondent:**
- [ ] Petition has been filed
- [ ] File-stamped copy of petition obtained
- [ ] Cause number assigned
- [ ] Respondent information verified

**For Execution:**
- [ ] Provide file-stamped petition copy to Respondent
- [ ] Explain waiver consequences
- [ ] Respondent reads and understands document
- [ ] Respondent signs voluntarily
- [ ] Notarization completed
- [ ] Original filed with court
- [ ] Copy retained for file

**After Execution:**
- [ ] File waiver with district clerk
- [ ] Note filing date
- [ ] Calendar any answer deadline (if not waived)

## Risk Flags

- [ ] Respondent signing without reading petition
- [ ] Respondent signing under duress or pressure
- [ ] Respondent lacks capacity to understand
- [ ] Waiver signed BEFORE petition filed (INVALID)
- [ ] Notarization errors
- [ ] Respondent later claims didn't understand

## Alternative: Waiver by Attorney

If Respondent is represented by counsel, the waiver may be signed by Respondent's attorney:

```
                    WAIVER OF SERVICE BY ATTORNEY

     As attorney for Respondent, {{RESPONDENT_NAME}}, I hereby
enter an appearance on behalf of Respondent for all purposes and
accept service on behalf of Respondent.

     I acknowledge receipt of a file-stamped copy of the Original
Petition for Divorce filed in this cause.


                              ___________________________________
                              {{RESPONDENT_ATTORNEY_NAME}}
                              State Bar No. {{RESPONDENT_ATTORNEY_BAR_NO}}
                              Attorney for Respondent
```
