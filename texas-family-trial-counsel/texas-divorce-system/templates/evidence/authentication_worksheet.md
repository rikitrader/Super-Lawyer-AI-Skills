## AUTHENTICATION OVERVIEW

### TRE 901(a) - General Requirement
To satisfy authentication, the proponent must produce evidence sufficient to support a finding that the item is what the proponent claims it is.

### TRE 901(b) - Methods of Authentication

| Method | Rule | Use For |
|--------|------|---------|
| Testimony of Witness with Knowledge | 901(b)(1) | Any item witness can identify |
| Non-Expert Handwriting Opinion | 901(b)(2) | Handwriting based on familiarity |
| Comparison by Expert | 901(b)(3) | Expert comparison to authenticated specimen |
| Distinctive Characteristics | 901(b)(4) | Emails, texts, unique features |
| Voice Identification | 901(b)(5) | Audio recordings |
| Telephone Conversation | 901(b)(6) | Calls identified by circumstances |
| Public Records | 901(b)(7) | Government records |
| Ancient Documents | 901(b)(8) | 20+ years old, proper custody |
| Process or System | 901(b)(9) | Computer-generated evidence |
| Statute/Rule | 901(b)(10) | Methods provided by law |

### TRE 902 - Self-Authenticating Documents

| Type | Rule | Examples |
|------|------|----------|
| Domestic Public Documents Under Seal | 902(1) | Court orders, certified records |
| Domestic Public Documents Not Under Seal | 902(2) | With certification |
| Foreign Public Documents | 902(3) | With certification |
| Certified Copies of Public Records | 902(4) | Deeds, vital records |
| Official Publications | 902(5) | Government publications |
| Newspapers and Periodicals | 902(6) | Published news |
| Trade Inscriptions | 902(7) | Labels, tags, signs |
| Acknowledged Documents | 902(8) | Notarized documents |
| Commercial Paper | 902(9) | Checks, notes |
| Business Records with Certification | 902(10) | Affidavit of custodian |

## SPECIFIC AUTHENTICATION GUIDES

### Text Messages

**Method:** Distinctive Characteristics (TRE 901(b)(4)) + Testimony

**Foundation Questions:**
```
Q: Do you recognize this document?
A: Yes, these are text messages between me and [person].

Q: How do you recognize them?
A: I recognize my phone number, [person]'s phone number, and the
   content of the conversation.

Q: Is this a fair and accurate representation of those text messages?
A: Yes.

Q: How were these captured/printed?
A: [Screenshot from my phone / printed from my phone / etc.]
```

**Additional Evidence to Support:**
- [ ] Phone records showing phone numbers
- [ ] Content references known facts
- [ ] Response to prior message
- [ ] Witness to receiving/sending

---

### Emails

**Method:** Distinctive Characteristics (TRE 901(b)(4)) + Testimony

**Foundation Questions:**
```
Q: Do you recognize this document?
A: Yes, this is an email I [sent/received].

Q: How do you recognize it?
A: I recognize my email address, [sender's] email address, and the
   subject line and content.

Q: Is the email address [address] your email address?
A: Yes.

Q: Is the email address [address] [person's] email address?
A: Yes, I have communicated with [person] at that address many times.

Q: Is this a fair and accurate copy of the email?
A: Yes.
```

---

### Social Media Posts

**Method:** Distinctive Characteristics + Testimony + Screenshot

**Foundation Questions:**
```
Q: Do you recognize this document?
A: Yes, this is a screenshot of a post from [person]'s [platform] account.

Q: How do you know this is [person]'s account?
A: I am familiar with [person]'s account. I have [friended/followed]
   that account. The profile picture and username match [person].

Q: When was this screenshot taken?
A: On [date].

Q: Did you take the screenshot yourself?
A: Yes.

Q: Is this a fair and accurate representation of what appeared on
   [platform] at that time?
A: Yes.
```

**Corroborating Evidence:**
- [ ] Account settings page showing account owner
- [ ] Other identifying information on page
- [ ] Content references unique facts

---

### Photographs

**Method:** Witness Testimony (TRE 901(b)(1))

**Foundation Questions:**
```
Q: I'm showing you [Party]'s Exhibit [#]. Do you recognize this photograph?
A: Yes.

Q: What does it depict?
A: [Description].

Q: How do you recognize what's in the photograph?
A: I was there when it was taken / I recognize [people/places/things].

Q: Does this photograph fairly and accurately represent [subject]
   as it appeared on [date/time]?
A: Yes.
```

---

### Audio/Video Recordings

**Method:** Witness Testimony + Voice Identification (TRE 901(b)(5))

**Foundation Questions:**
```
Q: I'm going to play [Party]'s Exhibit [#]. Please listen/watch.
   [Play recording]

Q: Do you recognize the voice(s) on this recording?
A: Yes.

Q: Whose voice is it?
A: [Person's name].

Q: How do you recognize the voice?
A: I have spoken with [person] many times and am familiar with
   [his/her] voice.

Q: Is this recording a fair and accurate representation of the
   conversation that occurred?
A: Yes.

Q: Has this recording been altered or edited in any way?
A: No.
```

---

### Business Records

**Method:** TRE 803(6) + 902(10) Affidavit

**Foundation Questions (Live Witness):**
```
Q: What is your position with [Company]?
A: I am the [records custodian/manager/etc.].

Q: Are you familiar with the record-keeping practices of [Company]?
A: Yes.

Q: I'm showing you [Party]'s Exhibit [#]. Do you recognize this document?
A: Yes, this is a [type of record] from [Company].

Q: Is this record kept in the regular course of [Company]'s business?
A: Yes.

Q: Is it the regular practice of [Company] to make and keep such records?
A: Yes.

Q: Was this record made at or near the time of the events recorded?
A: Yes.

Q: Was it made by or from information transmitted by a person with
   knowledge?
A: Yes.
```

**Alternative: Affidavit Method (TRE 902(10))**
- [ ] Obtain records with certified affidavit
- [ ] File notice of intent to use
- [ ] Serve on opposing party 14+ days before trial
