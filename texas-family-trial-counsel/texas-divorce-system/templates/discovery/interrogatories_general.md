     [PETITIONER'S/RESPONDENT'S] FIRST SET OF INTERROGATORIES
                    TO [RESPONDENT/PETITIONER]

TO: {{OPPOSING_PARTY_NAME}}, [Petitioner/Respondent], by and through
    [his/her] attorney of record, {{OPPOSING_COUNSEL_NAME}}

     Pursuant to Rules 197 of the Texas Rules of Civil Procedure,
you are requested to answer separately and fully, in writing under
oath, each of the following interrogatories within thirty (30) days
after service hereof.

                            DEFINITIONS

     1. "You" or "Your" refers to {{OPPOSING_PARTY_NAME}}, including
agents, employees, representatives, and attorneys.

     2. "Document" means writings, drawings, graphs, charts,
photographs, recordings, and other data compilations from which
information can be obtained.

     3. "Communication" means any exchange of information, whether
oral or written, including but not limited to letters, emails, text
messages, and conversations.

     4. "Person" includes natural persons, corporations, partnerships,
associations, and any other legal entities.

     5. "Identify" when used with respect to a person means to state
the person's full name, last known address, telephone number, and
relationship to you.

     6. "Identify" when used with respect to a document means to
state the date, author, recipient, type of document, and general
subject matter.

     7. "Marriage" refers to the marriage between {{PETITIONER_NAME}}
and {{RESPONDENT_NAME}}.

     8. "Children" refers to the minor child(ren) of the marriage:
{{CHILD_1_NAME}}[, {{CHILD_2_NAME}}].

                          INSTRUCTIONS

     1. These interrogatories are continuing in nature, and you are
required to supplement your answers as provided by the Texas Rules
of Civil Procedure.

     2. If you object to any interrogatory, state the specific ground
for objection and answer to the extent not objectionable.

     3. If you claim any privilege, identify the privilege claimed
and the factual basis for the claim.

                         INTERROGATORIES

              SECTION A: IDENTIFICATION AND BACKGROUND

INTERROGATORY NO. 1:
     State your full legal name, all names by which you have been
known, your date of birth, Social Security number, and driver's
license number.

INTERROGATORY NO. 2:
     State all addresses at which you have resided during the past
five (5) years, including dates of residence at each address.

INTERROGATORY NO. 3:
     Identify all persons currently residing with you, stating their
names, ages, and relationship to you.


              SECTION B: EMPLOYMENT AND INCOME

INTERROGATORY NO. 4:
     State the name and address of your current employer, your job
title, your date of hire, and your current gross and net monthly
income.

INTERROGATORY NO. 5:
     Identify all employers you have had during the past five (5)
years, stating for each: the employer's name and address, your job
title, dates of employment, and reason for leaving.

INTERROGATORY NO. 6:
     State all sources of income you have received during the past
twelve (12) months, including but not limited to wages, bonuses,
commissions, self-employment income, rental income, interest,
dividends, trust distributions, gifts, and any other income.

INTERROGATORY NO. 7:
     If you are self-employed or own any interest in a business,
identify the business, describe your ownership interest, state the
gross revenues and net income for the past three (3) years, and
identify all persons with knowledge of the business finances.

INTERROGATORY NO. 8:
     State whether you have the ability to earn more income than you
currently earn, and if so, explain why you are not earning that income.


              SECTION C: PROPERTY AND ASSETS

INTERROGATORY NO. 9:
     Identify all real property in which you have any interest,
stating for each: the address, the date acquired, how title is held,
the current fair market value, and any mortgage or lien balance.

INTERROGATORY NO. 10:
     Identify all bank accounts, including checking, savings, money
market, and certificate of deposit accounts, in which you have or
have had any interest during the marriage, stating for each: the
financial institution, account number, type of account, names on
the account, and current balance.

INTERROGATORY NO. 11:
     Identify all retirement accounts, including 401(k), IRA, pension,
and deferred compensation, in which you have any interest, stating
for each: the plan name, account number, current balance, and any
loans against the account.

INTERROGATORY NO. 12:
     Identify all vehicles (automobiles, trucks, motorcycles, boats,
recreational vehicles) in which you have any interest, stating for
each: the year, make, model, VIN, how title is held, current value,
and any loan balance.

INTERROGATORY NO. 13:
     Identify all life insurance policies on your life or on which
you are the owner or beneficiary, stating for each: the insurance
company, policy number, face value, cash value, owner, and beneficiary.

INTERROGATORY NO. 14:
     Identify all stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and other investment
accounts in which you have any interest, stating for each: the
institution, account number, type of investment, and current value.

INTERROGATORY NO. 15:
     Describe any interest you have in any business entity, including
corporations, partnerships, LLCs, or sole proprietorships, stating
the name, your ownership percentage, and estimated value.


              SECTION D: DEBTS AND LIABILITIES

INTERROGATORY NO. 16:
     Identify all debts and liabilities you owe, stating for each:
the creditor's name, account number, current balance, monthly payment,
and how the debt was incurred.

INTERROGATORY NO. 17:
     State whether you have incurred any debt since the date of
separation, and if so, identify each such debt.


              SECTION E: CHILDREN AND PARENTING

INTERROGATORY NO. 18:
     Describe in detail your typical weekly schedule with the
child(ren), including morning routines, transportation to school,
after-school activities, dinner, homework, and bedtime.

INTERROGATORY NO. 19:
     Identify all childcare providers who have cared for the child(ren)
during the past two (2) years, stating their names, addresses, dates
of service, and cost.

INTERROGATORY NO. 20:
     Identify all medical providers, including doctors, dentists,
therapists, and counselors, who have treated the child(ren) during
the past two (2) years.

INTERROGATORY NO. 21:
     Describe any special needs of the child(ren) and identify any
services or treatment currently being provided.

INTERROGATORY NO. 22:
     State your proposed custody and possession arrangement for the
child(ren), including the reasons why you believe this arrangement
is in the child(ren)'s best interest.


              SECTION F: MARRIAGE AND SEPARATION

INTERROGATORY NO. 23:
     State the date and location of your marriage to {{PETITIONER_NAME/
RESPONDENT_NAME}}.

INTERROGATORY NO. 24:
     State the date you and your spouse physically separated and
describe the circumstances of separation.

INTERROGATORY NO. 25:
     State the grounds upon which you seek a divorce or upon which
you contend a divorce should be granted.


              SECTION G: WITNESSES

INTERROGATORY NO. 26:
     Identify all persons who have knowledge of facts relevant to
this case, stating for each: their name, address, telephone number,
and the subject matter of their knowledge.
