## DEPOSITION OUTLINE

### I. PRELIMINARY MATTERS

**A. Introduction and Ground Rules**

```
Q: Please state your full legal name for the record.
Q: Have you ever gone by any other names?
Q: What is your date of birth?
Q: What is your current address?

Q: Have you ever had your deposition taken before?
   [If yes: In what case? When?]

Q: Do you understand that you are under oath today, just as
   if you were testifying in court?

Q: Do you understand that you must answer my questions verbally
   so the court reporter can take down your answers?

Q: If you don't understand a question, will you ask me to
   rephrase it?

Q: If you answer a question, I will assume you understood it.
   Is that fair?

Q: Are you taking any medication or substances today that would
   affect your ability to understand my questions and answer
   truthfully?

Q: Is there any reason you cannot give truthful testimony today?
```

**B. Preparation**

```
Q: How did you prepare for this deposition?
Q: Did you meet with your attorney?
Q: What documents did you review?
Q: Did anyone tell you what questions I might ask?
Q: Did anyone tell you how to answer any questions?
```

---

### II. BACKGROUND AND EMPLOYMENT

**A. Current Employment**

```
Q: Are you currently employed?
Q: What is the name of your employer?
Q: What is your job title?
Q: How long have you been in this position?
Q: Describe your job duties.
Q: What is your current salary or hourly rate?
Q: How are you paid - weekly, biweekly, monthly?
Q: What is your gross monthly income from this job?
Q: Do you receive any bonuses? [If yes: How much? How often?]
Q: Do you receive any commissions? [If yes: How much?]
Q: Do you have any stock options or equity compensation?
Q: What benefits do you receive?
Q: Do you have the ability to earn overtime? [If yes: How much?]
```

**B. Employment History**

```
Q: Where were you employed before your current job?
Q: Why did you leave that position?
Q: [Continue for past 5 years]

Q: Have you ever been fired or asked to resign from a job?
Q: Have you ever been demoted?
Q: Have you ever been disciplined at work?
```

**C. Other Income**

```
Q: Do you have any other sources of income besides your employment?
Q: Do you receive any rental income?
Q: Do you receive any interest or dividend income?
Q: Do you receive any income from a business?
Q: Do you receive any income from a trust?
Q: Have you received any gifts of money in the past year?
```

---

### III. FINANCIAL MATTERS

**A. Bank Accounts**

```
Q: What bank accounts do you currently have?
Q: [For each account:]
   - What is the name of the bank?
   - What type of account is it?
   - Whose name is on the account?
   - What is the current balance?
   - Do you have online access?

Q: Have you closed any bank accounts since [separation date]?
Q: Have you opened any new accounts since [separation date]?
Q: Have you transferred money between accounts since [separation date]?
Q: Have you withdrawn large sums of cash since [separation date]?
```

**B. Retirement Accounts**

```
Q: Do you have any retirement accounts?
Q: [For each account:]
   - What type of account is it?
   - What company is it with?
   - What is the current balance?
   - Have you taken any loans against it?
   - Have you taken any withdrawals?
   - Who is the beneficiary?
```

**C. Debts**

```
Q: What credit cards do you have?
Q: What is the balance on each?
Q: Have you incurred any new debt since [separation date]?
Q: What did you charge on credit cards since [separation date]?
Q: Do you owe any personal loans?
Q: Do you owe any money to family members?
```

---

### IV. PROPERTY

**A. Real Property**

```
Q: Do you own any real estate?
Q: [For each property:]
   - What is the address?
   - When was it purchased?
   - How is title held?
   - What is it worth?
   - What is owed on it?
   - Who lives there?
   - Who pays the mortgage?
```

**B. Personal Property**

```
Q: What vehicles do you own or drive?
Q: What is each vehicle worth?
Q: Is there a loan on any vehicle?

Q: Do you own any valuable personal property?
   [Jewelry, art, collections, equipment]

Q: Have you sold or given away any property since [separation date]?
```

**C. Business Interests**

```
Q: Do you own any interest in any business?
Q: What is the name of the business?
Q: What does the business do?
Q: What is your ownership percentage?
Q: What is the business worth?
Q: How much income do you receive from the business?
Q: Who else has an ownership interest?
```

---

### V. CHILDREN AND PARENTING

**A. Current Arrangement**

```
Q: Describe your current custody arrangement.
Q: Where do the children live?
Q: How much time do you spend with the children?
Q: Describe a typical weekday with the children.
Q: Describe a typical weekend with the children.
```

**B. Parenting Involvement**

```
Q: Who gets the children ready for school in the morning?
Q: Who takes the children to school?
Q: Who picks the children up from school?
Q: Who helps with homework?
Q: Who prepares meals for the children?
Q: Who puts the children to bed?
Q: Who takes the children to doctor appointments?
Q: Who attends parent-teacher conferences?
Q: Who attends the children's activities and events?
```

**C. Childcare**

```
Q: Who provides childcare when you are at work?
Q: What does childcare cost?
Q: Who pays for childcare?
```

**D. Proposed Arrangement**

```
Q: What custody arrangement are you asking for?
Q: Why do you believe this is in the children's best interest?
Q: What concerns do you have about [my client]'s parenting?
Q: What would you do differently as a parent?
```

---

### VI. MARRIAGE AND SEPARATION

**A. Marriage History**

```
Q: When did you and [Petitioner/Respondent] get married?
Q: When did you first start having marital problems?
Q: What were the problems?
Q: Did you seek marriage counseling?
Q: When did you decide to get divorced?
Q: Who made the decision to separate?
```

**B. Grounds/Fault Issues**

```
[If alleging fault:]
Q: Are you claiming [cruelty/adultery/other]?
Q: Describe the specific conduct you are relying on.
Q: When did this occur?
Q: Who witnessed this?
Q: Did you tell anyone about this at the time?
Q: Did you seek medical treatment?
Q: Did you call the police?
```

---

### VII. CREDIBILITY AND IMPEACHMENT

**A. Prior Statements**

```
[Use interrogatory responses, documents, social media]

Q: Do you recall answering interrogatories in this case?
Q: Were your answers true and accurate?
Q: I'm showing you your answer to Interrogatory No. [X].
   Did you give this answer?
Q: Is this answer true?

[If inconsistent with current testimony:]
Q: Today you testified [X]. In your interrogatories you stated [Y].
   Which is correct?
```

**B. Social Media/Communications**

```
Q: Do you have a Facebook account?
Q: Do you have an Instagram account?
Q: Have you posted anything about this divorce?
Q: Have you posted anything about [my client]?
Q: Have you posted anything about your lifestyle or activities?

[Show specific posts if relevant]
```

---

### VIII. CLOSING

```
Q: Is there anything you want to add or clarify?
Q: Have you answered all my questions truthfully?
Q: Is there anything that would change any of your answers?
```
