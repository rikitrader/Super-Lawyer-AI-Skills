## Subpoena Requirements Overview

### TRCP 176 - Subpoenas

**Subpoena Authority:**
- May command attendance at hearing/trial
- May command production of documents
- May command inspection of premises
- Issued by clerk of court or attorney

### TRCP 205 - Discovery from Non-Parties

**Notice Requirement:**
- Must serve notice on all parties
- Notice at least 10 days before subpoena served
- Must include copy of subpoena
- Parties may object to scope

## Subpoena Process Workflow

### Step 1: Prepare Notice to Parties (TRCP 205.2)

```
NOTICE OF INTENT TO SERVE SUBPOENA ON NON-PARTY

TO: All parties and counsel of record

     Please take notice that {{REQUESTING_PARTY}} intends to serve
a subpoena duces tecum on the following non-party:

     Name: {{THIRD_PARTY_NAME}}
     Address: {{THIRD_PARTY_ADDRESS}}

     Documents Requested: [Description of documents]

     A copy of the subpoena is attached.

     Date: {{DATE}}

     {{ATTORNEY_NAME}}
```

- [ ] Notice prepared
- [ ] Copy of subpoena attached
- [ ] Served on all parties at least 10 days before subpoena served

---

### Step 2: Prepare Subpoena

**Subpoena Content:**
- [ ] Caption and cause number
- [ ] Name and address of custodian
- [ ] Description of documents requested
- [ ] Time period for records
- [ ] Production deadline and location
- [ ] Clerk signature/seal (or attorney signature)

---

### Step 3: Issue Subpoena

**Options:**
- [ ] Obtain from district clerk (with seal)
- [ ] Issue as attorney under TRCP 176.4

---

### Step 4: Serve Subpoena

**Service Requirements:**
- Personal service on custodian
- Or as permitted by TRCP 176.5
- Include witness fee and mileage (if personal appearance)

## Sample Subpoena Language

### Bank Records

```
All documents relating to any account in which {{ACCOUNT_HOLDER}}
has or had any interest from {{START_DATE}} to the present, including:

1. Account statements;
2. Cancelled checks (front and back);
3. Deposit items;
4. Wire transfer records;
5. Account applications and signature cards;
6. Safe deposit box access records;
7. Loan documents and statements.
```

### Employment Records

```
All documents relating to the employment of {{EMPLOYEE_NAME}},
including:

1. Personnel file;
2. Employment application;
3. Pay records from {{START_DATE}} to present;
4. W-2 forms for {{YEARS}};
5. Bonus, commission, and incentive records;
6. Stock option and equity compensation records;
7. Benefits enrollment and statements;
8. Expense reimbursement records.
```

## Completion Checklist

**Before Issuing:**
- [ ] Notice to parties prepared
- [ ] Notice served (10 days before subpoena)
- [ ] Subpoena prepared
- [ ] Records description specific
- [ ] Time period appropriate
- [ ] Compliance deadline reasonable

**For Service:**
- [ ] Subpoena issued (clerk or attorney)
- [ ] Witness fee included (if appearance required)
- [ ] Served on custodian
- [ ] Proof of service obtained

**After Service:**
- [ ] Compliance deadline calendared
- [ ] Follow-up system in place
- [ ] Documents organized when received
- [ ] Deficiencies addressed

## Risk Flags

- [ ] Notice to parties not timely
- [ ] Subpoena overly broad
- [ ] HIPAA/FERPA compliance issues
- [ ] Privilege issues (e.g., therapy records)
- [ ] Third party objects
- [ ] Compliance deadline too short
